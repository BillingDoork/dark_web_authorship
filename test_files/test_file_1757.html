2 days from the time it's *actually* shipped. a lot of yahoos on here will mark stuff in transit and sit on it for a day or more, or mail things after the post office closes, delaying it another day, or more if said day is a saturday.

mail is moved and sorted 7 days a week, just not accepted or delivered on sunday.

shipped monday ---> delivered wednsday
shipped tuesday --> delivered thursday
shipped wednsday -> delivered friday
shipped thursday --> delivered saturday
shipped friday -------> delivered monday
shipped saturday ---> delivered monday
