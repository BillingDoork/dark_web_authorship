Post by: Joeyjojojr on June 02, 2012, 04:59 pm
Thats good about your refund rate. Another couple of pointers that have helped me along the road:

-If something looks to good to be true, it probably is.
-Read the vendors feedbacks. If they all seem like they were made by the same person (a lot of times the vendor himself) and the vendor is new, it could be a scam and proceed with caution.
-Even established legit vendors sometimes flip and decide to cut and run so be prepared for that.
-Sucks to say it, but if a vendor requires you to finalize early, dont spend more money than you would be willing to lose. It can be a gamble sometimes and again vendors could cut and run.
-You are new, so you are probably going to have to finalize early, but avoid doing this if at all possible.
-Get some smaller orders under your belt before ordering a sheet for instance.
-If you Finalize before you receive your product you are not protected by SR and stand to lose your btc.
-Research on the forums. The more you know the better your chances of having a successful transaction.
-If you have questions ask the vendors or ask here on the forums. This is a great community that wants to help out fellow members.
-Always be respectful to the vendors and understand that they get bombarded with questions so it may take time before they get back to you.
I could go on, but half of the fun is learning this stuff for yourself. One more thing:
-Have fun and enjoy. This place is really great.

Peace!
