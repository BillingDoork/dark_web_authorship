Flat Rate boxes are free.  The only way to ship them anonymously is to use postage stamps and drop boxes.  Tracking can become an important issue regardless of how well you know the addressee(as in packages lost or missent while in the system)but that's up to you and your  buyers.  You can just as easily  place your own tracking labels onto flat rates providing you have them.

Also, before dropping stamped boxes be sure to educate yourself on the PO's 13 Ounce Rule.
