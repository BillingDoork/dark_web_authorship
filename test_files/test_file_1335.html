I had planned on ordering to that box as soon as I got back from the post office, but my paranoid side was not satisfied with how things played out. The entire conversation was friendly, but I knew I screwed up. I shouldn't have said anything really because it wasn't a serious question, but I did a good job staying cool and delivered my response well. I plan on getting legitimate mail to my PO Box to cover up my packages from SR. The question is should I wait 2 weeks or so for my order or is it safe to go ahead and order now?
