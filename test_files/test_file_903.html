Post by: Wadozo on June 22, 2012, 10:23 am
There is no doubt that the Netherlands is a universally,  well known hot spot for exporting drugs to countries all over the world. However, I have to agree with some of the others who have posted on this thread in regards to there being a lot of speculation, assumptions and innuendo which continues to cloud the issue of some packages not arriving from the Netherlands. There may be an increased level of security shown to packages from the Netherlands which would no doubt make it more difficult to get things through Customs, however, in scenarios such as this, the vendors really need to "up the anti" and refine their shipping methods and even approach things from a  totally different perspective. Look at purchasing some newer or even superior quality packaging materials in the hope of improving the professionalism of their packaging and not attracting the attention of Customs. For me, the time and effort a vendor spends on packaging your purchase safely and securely, being careful not to leave any trace of an illegal substance on the package itself (and the contents vac.sealed and secured in stealth) , which  minimizes any unwanted attention a drug dog may show, and the professionalism shown by vendors in producing "business"style letters with all the labeling professionally printed on and not hand written , which helps it blend in beautifully with the everyday mail, are the major contributors in your package making it from the Netherlands to the country it is destined for. From my experience on SR, all my orders dispatched from the Netherlands have arrived to Oz quickly and safely, as the quality of the packaging was just superb. :) :)
