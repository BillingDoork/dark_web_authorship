Title: Re: Is the delivery date on tracked packages accurate?
Post by: harpua25 on July 14, 2012, 08:59 pm
You mean a delivery confirmation number from the USPS?  USPS stresses that's not actually a tracking number, per se, and in my experience it's often off by a day.  Actual tracking numbers from FedEx and other couriers are usually pretty accurate, though.
Title: Re: Is the delivery date on tracked packages accurate?
Post by: gracevan3569 on July 14, 2012, 10:28 pm
It depends on what website you are using. I saw a link posted on the forums that could be safely accessed via TOR and it was off by a day or more or movement.  I went USPS website via clearnet and it was accurate. Often you won't get but 1-2 updates each day (as in 1. processed in X sort facility and 2. out for delivery) but it is updated fairly regularly in my experience.  It is not up to the minute but each day should give you some indication of progress.
