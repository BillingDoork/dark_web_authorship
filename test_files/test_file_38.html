Everyone has different opinions on this and it basically comes down to whatever option you're most comfortable with. Go with whatever option you feel safe with and aren't going to be freaking out/losing sleep over. In my situation I just use my regular address and name, that way I know as long as the vendor ships my item I will be receiving it....I just always make sure to encrypt my address when placing an order.

When it comes to what name to use...a lot of vendors recommend that you use your real name because if you don't then there's a chance you won't receive your item....and there's plenty of threads already on here with buyers complaining because their item wasn't delivered due to them using a fake name or a past tenants name.
