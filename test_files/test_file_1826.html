Post by: cacoethes on July 19, 2012, 07:40 pm
Don't overthink the delivery aspect of this thing to the exclusion of everything else.

The weak point in the whole transaction is the delivery point.  You must at some point physically possess the package or it does you no good.  Fake IDs, PO boxes, vacant mailboxes, and aliases will only obfuscate the obvious, not shield you from prosecution or protect you from being charged.

If you are ever charged, there are really only a few very simple details you need to have worked out...

1) Have a good attorney
2) Be absolutely positive that there is NO OTHER EVIDENCE that can be used to prove you had ANY PRIOR KNOWLEDGE AT ALL of the contraband that was mailed to you.  Nothing in your house.  Nothing on your computer.  Nothing on your phone.  NOTHING AT ALL.  You should also make sure your home is clean of any other drugs/paraphernalia.
3) Keep your mouth shut.  Do not deny anything, do not offer any explanation at all.  Just shut up and lawyer up.

Fake names and addresses won't help your cause.  False identification and PO boxes obtained with such credentials won't help either, but they will give the feds something else to charge you with, and the evidence they need to convict you, too.

This whole thing works because the USPS processes roughly 200,000,000,000 pieces of mail annually- that's about 20,000,000 pieces of mail per HOUR.  Don't make it more complicated than it needs to be.
