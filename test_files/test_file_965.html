Title: Re: Shipping from the Post Office
Post by: NCChronic on April 18, 2013, 02:39 am
go on usps.com and look up Self-Service Kiosks in your area. Just BEWARE of the camera pointed at your face whenyou use it, I cover it the entire time when using.
With that kiosk ou can select the type of box, flat rate etc. then put in the zipcode of the addressee, then use a gift card visa, buy at Walgreens or something.
You can purchase up to 8 as credit, then it won't take that visa without a pin number. So I always have two cards available.

Put the label onto your box, then your printed label from home with the destination address, and have a return label as well. I print everything out and take it with me to PO, all boxes are have the zipcode on them so I know which box goes to who.
THats it. done. and everyone has made it to the destination. BUT now I use the click n ship, PM if you want to know.
