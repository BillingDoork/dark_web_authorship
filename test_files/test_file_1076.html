Post by: Squirrel on May 08, 2013, 04:51 am
This is completely my opinion (not really) so take that with a grain of salt.  The USPS (if that's what your dealing with) has so many problems right now, the associates at the package handling centers and the carriers have zero incentive to play detective.  Now your mail may be going through big hubs where there may be random dogs and/or x-rays but these are usually used for imports from other countries and to inspect suspicious packaging.  The average postman/mail handler has little interest in the numerous packages you get at your house and, at least in my case, has even engendered a little closer relationship with said mail carrier.  I talk with him all the time even though my dog wants to jump through the screen and eat him.  Now, having said all that, what are the things (in my mind) that are going to get you busted?

1-Shipments with poor stealth from lazy vendor.

2-Vendor who does not truly destroy his/her records like they're supposed to and HE/SHE ends up taking you to jail with him/her when his/her shipments are tracked all over the world.

3-Stupidity on your part.  Do you really need a QP of weed or 7 oz's of coke?  Not if you're just a user so don't fall into the temptation of being greedy.

Remember, there are a lot of bigger fish to fry in the world right now.  LE may get to us eventually but for now, we're pretty safe.
