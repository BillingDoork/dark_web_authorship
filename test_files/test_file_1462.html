Get grounded.  Think about this from my perspective (not emotionally involved).  Here's what you know:

- You have packages that likely should have arrived by now.
- They have not.
- Your country is a generally risky/scrutinizing one.
- On the other hand, you chose your vendors carefully so it's unlikely you got scammed.

Three things can happen.  1. The packages all you show up tomorrow and you feel silly for worrying. 2. They got seized/lost and nothing LEO-related will happen.  This is a win-lose situation.  Your address may very well be flagged but it is far more important that you avoided any charges. 3. You've got a CD on your way.

If either 1 or 2 happen you'll be grumpy and somewhat broke or happy, but it's all over.  The only thing you can do about any of it is make the 3rd option less painful.  So clean your room.  Hide or have friends hold paraphernalia for you, or drugs themselves, if you have any. Keep a clean shop.  If they search your house, make sure there's nothing to find.

Don't get me wrong - I do think you don't have anything to worry about.  But all I'm saying is the only actionable thing you can do regardless of which outcome is coming other than to just wait is to minimize the potential damage of option 3.  Then sit back, relax, drink a beer, (I'm jealous already, I'm in AA ...) and enjoy your day. There's nothing else you can do.
