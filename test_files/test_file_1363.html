Title: Re: Is Tracking worth it?
Post by: MissNatural on July 02, 2013, 07:29 am
EU->USA I would highly recommend tracking, especially if you are a buyer, that is if you care about your money. If your order gets lost and there is no tracking, good luck getting the vendor to settle with a refund/reship, as there will be no proof you didn't receive it. So basically you can lose $6 for tracking, or you can lose $70-$100+ in your order in the case of a mishap, which I have had from EU->USA, most specifically UK->USA.

Tracking on packages will not be 'looked at' any more suspiciously than any other package. The only thing that will get your package looked at is it looking suspicious and your vendor being very crappy with his packaging. It's one of the reasons I only use vendors that have a few months of experience, or if the vendors tell me exactly how good their stealth is.
