Title: Re: Order frequency, and delivery rate?
Post by: cactuschomper on September 27, 2013, 02:22 am
I ordered about 50+ parcels to an address that was a total trap (in a 6month period.) After number 50something I ordered 1-Oz of Wax from Just4u2NV, and behold a love letter arrived instead of my wax. Thanks to J4U for reshipping, but it was quite surprising and my first experience. Usually their parcels arrived in 3-4biz days, this time it took about 5-6 before my postal inspection service letter arrived. Not too big of a deal, but enough to obviously stop all traffic and scrap the location within a week :P I'd say moral of the story, it depends a lot. Take in to account your location, the demographics of the area, vendor location, number of international vs domestic orders to your address, and I may be missing a couple others. All of these in conjunction can give you a pretty good idea when it's time to stop, or even time to get the hell out of dodge. Hope this experience helps!
