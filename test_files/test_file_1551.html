You're leaving too much detail out to be able to say anything for sure. To begin with you're not mentioning if you used your own ID, a fake one or none at all when registering with the Mail Forwarder itself, this obviously sets the stage for how it proceeds. Now I have to assume since you don't mention any details you didn't use any ID and are thus only able to use UPS/FedEx/DHL which all require signature to get your packages, these companies regularly get a lot of drugs sent through them and they will let alert Law Enforcement to any package instantly if they find it. If you had used an ID of some kind for the Mail Forwarder you could easily have been able to avoid having your address in the system as you wouldn't have to specify your address right away then. Another huge aspect in this is what kind of stealth the Vendor you bought from provides, if it's just 50 pills in a bottle which can easily be heard by shaking the package then that's obviously going to create a lot of suspicion.

Quote
I use the neighbors internet connection. Kind of paranoid because of houses in my country are really close like in japan. So it wouldnt be hard to find whos using the internet I guess?

If you ordered to your own name and address the point of what connection you used becomes moot, multiple Mail Forwarders let you store mail with them before they forward them but since you didn't pick this option you won't have any options on this matter. If you intend to do this in a similar way in the future I would suggest you use a VPN, perhaps one from the thread VPN Services That Take Your Anonymity Seriously, 2013 Edition: dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=177551
----------
I would also suggest you take a look in my thread Mail Forwarding and Virtual Office Providers - Concept and Thoughts: http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=161081

If you're going to use Mail Forwarders in what appears to be the most superficial way possible (ie. no ID required at first Mail Forwarder) you should at the very least send the Package to a secondary Mail Forwarder in your own country before you ship it to yourself, in that way you will at the very least know if it made it through Customs or not. Still I can't entirely blame you for thinking it would be this easy as technically it is, as long as nothing gets caught. :P
