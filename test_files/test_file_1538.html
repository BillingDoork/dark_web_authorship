Title: Re: Am I just being too paranoid?
Post by: bluedev1 on August 01, 2013, 07:41 am
Be patient.  After 21 days, then I would begin to wonder.

The way international registered mail works in most places is they fill up crates of mail going to the destination country.  Until the crate is full, it doesn't get sent.  So sometimes you'll get lucky and land at the top of the heap and sometimes not so lucky and there won't be a lot of mail for whatever reason.  That is why it varies so much internationally iirc.
