Vials are difficult to reliably get through customs so in my estimation the vast majority of steroids are sent to the US as powder and then made into vials in the US either for personal use or for resale.  The customs center you're going through makes a huge difference and many steroid distributors flat out refuse to ship packages that are going to go through the difficult customs centers (based on origin and destination zip code).  For the customs center that is more difficult it is important to at least ship in smaller quantities to lessen the probability of a controlled delivery for your customers.

My experience is that if customs finds liquid using the x-ray then they are doing to check the package.  I don't know much about x-ray so I'm unsure about liquid x-ray stealth.  It does seem like dry vials get through easier than liquid vials though.
