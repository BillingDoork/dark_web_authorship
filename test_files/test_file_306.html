Post by: quinone on October 27, 2012, 09:02 am
I've had orders from addresses that are a 20 minute drive from me take 5+ day's to arrive, so if I were you I wouldn't be too worried, USPS etc. is just sucking balls cuz of cutbacks recession whatever bullshit.

microRNA is also riight, many vendors will put your order into processing ... long before they actually ship it.  Of course this is a STUPID thing for vendors to do, but many of them do it, so he coulda put it into processing at the beginning of the week and just not put it in the mail until the end of the week (sloppy and annoying ! )

You should ask the vendor to check the DCN of your order and see what it's status is.

If something is seized you will receive a letter, they won't setup a controlled delivery based on the first seizure at an address (esp. if its a small quantity ... they might flag that address though), it's expensive to their (LE) budget to setup sting's so their only going to target addresses that have either had a few seizures, or the seizure was for a large quantity.

I'd say LE is least of your worries, you either got scammed by the vendor, a postal worker stole it from you, it fell off a conveyor or was misrouted to the wrong bin at the sorting facility (in which case you'll still get it ... it'll just take until they find it) or it was shipped to the wrong address (either by you mistyping you're own address or the vendor being sloppy and not copying your address letter for letter).

You'll get more answers out of the vendor then the forums so you should ask him to check the DCN

I PERSONALLY would hold off on ordering to that address, but that's only cuz I have many sucessful transactions, so one that's just taking way to look would seem suspect to me.  It's different in your case if your a new buyer though, it could just be the USPS centers in your region and how bad they are compared to other areas.

Don't get paranoid though, if they seized it they will most definitely send you a love letter if this is the first seizure to that address.
