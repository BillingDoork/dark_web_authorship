Title: Re: general questions
Post by: quinone on August 21, 2012, 03:18 am
Most vendors will charge you for shipping because it cost them money to buy packaging materials and pay for the shipping itself.  Many offer free shipping though, perhaps as an incentive for you to buy from them, or they can just afford it cuz the profit they're making trumps the shipping cost.

Whose mailbox it ends up in is irrelevant aside from the security aspect of having packages sent to an address that's not your own and the increased risks involved (and from that the increased number of reship's/refunds the vendor has to make).
