this is a typical sequence of events when a letter/package arrives in Canada from abroad:

date/city: International item arrived in Canada and will be reviewed by Customs
date/city: Item released and processed at local Canada Post delivery facility
date/city: Item out for delivery

This happens usually in a day or two the max.

I've had an envelope sit over two weeks in customs in Vancouver.
