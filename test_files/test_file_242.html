Hello everyone

I just got back from the post office (USA) buying Priority Mail stamps.  The lady working at the counter told me the rate is going up.  Effective January 27, 2013 Priority Mail Flat Rate goes to:

Small Box:  $5.80
Medium Box:  $12.35
Large Box:  $16.85
Large APO/FPO Box:  $14.85
Regular Envelope:  $5.60
Legal Envelope:  $5.75
Padded Envelope:  $5.95

Also, First-Class Mail goes to:

Letters up to 1oz:  $.46
Letters to international destinations:  $1.10
Postcards:  $.33

A list she gave me says the Postal Regulatory Commission will review the prices before they become effective.  The new Mailing Service prices will be available at http://pe.usps.com
