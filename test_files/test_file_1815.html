Title: Re: Risks of receiving small batches of acid
Post by: raven92 on July 20, 2012, 08:55 am
Quote from: darkmirror on July 20, 2012, 08:40 am
I would think blotter would be one of the easiest to get in. In the middle of a wallet perhaps?  I wouldn't think body heat would break it down to much if you put it deep in your wallet

Oxygen and UV break down LSD, the hotter it gets the quicker these reactions begin to occur. Heat by itself is a marginal factor, in fact there was a vile of crystal kept at varying temperatures reaching far above above room temperature for over 20 years with nearly no loss of potency. Of course it was in a sealed amber vial.

As far as penalty, LSD is probably one of the worst you can get caught with, there have been raids for tiny personal amounts. Luckily its one of the easiest to get shipped, if the shipper is smart it can be hidden well, sadly most SR vendors ship it in ways if anyone opened the package it would be blatantly obvious it was a blotter of some substance. White or printed if there is a tiny strip of thick white paper in a ziplock or vac sealed you'd have to be a moron not to know what it is. Right now there are a lot of scams going around, and I know nothing of china's import regulations, I'd wager to guess they are pretty rigid.
