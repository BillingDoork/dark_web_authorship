While that approach would protect you if the item was being watched from the original sender (in your example, Turkey), though only if your buddy in "Greece" would be willing to take the rap for you on 3 kilo's (I need more friends like that, maybe should start a religion),  you actually quadruple your exposure/risk.

Think about it, if the package was sent from Turkey to Deutschland the exposure would be the people processing it at the drop off point, at the hub it is sent to, at the hub it's received at, and finally the person actually delivering it.  Now if you sent it to all those places the package would go through that same process 4 times instead of once, not to mention the legitimate risk of each of your four buddies picking it up and transporting it (not to mention them deciding to keep it and tell you it was lost).
