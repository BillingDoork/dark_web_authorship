As far as my memory goes from reading an FBI document that was floating around somewhere (can't remember which one sorry, I think it was case file about a bust they did) they tend to reseal intercept post they want to try and catch you for so I imagine they'd use new packaging etc, the solution is to know what type of packaging your seller uses (although the problem also comes that vendors change packaging often and some types of large company produced packaging are easy to find anywhere and so easy to remake exactly the same)

So there isn't really an obvious solution (as with all types of problems related to this otherwise we wouldn't be discussing it would we) My advice is, they don't care about personal amounts so if you're ordering a gram or ten you're probably fine. If you're ordering larger amounts, know your postman, know your sellers packaging, correspond with them about what it's being sent in so you know in advance and do not sign for anything you shouldn't be signing for.
