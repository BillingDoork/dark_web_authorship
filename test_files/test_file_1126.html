If you're a beginner wait a few months before becoming a seller, you want to see what other sellers are using as packages.. For fingerprints it's important you wear gloves when you touch to anything you are going to ship, some thing are hard to manipulate with gloves but you can use small pliers to manipulate them. You can get packaging supply from all office oriented shops and you doesn't need to go to the post office at all to send a package, just drop it in a nearby mail drop point (not the one that are the closest to your house) you have to weight the packages yourself with a scale but this is damn easy. A good thing is to vac seal everything too, depending on the drug you are wanting to ship, some sellers double or triple vac seal the stuff they ship to evade sniffing dogs or electronic sniffers.
