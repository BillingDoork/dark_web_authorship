Title: UK Royal mail

here is a little info for the UK resident
some of you might know that already
here is a message I got from Royal mail

Dear Mr silkroad

Thank you for contacting Royal Mail regarding mail to be delivered to you in the UK.

I can advise that within the terms of our operating licence, Royal Mail is legally obliged to deliver items as they have been addressed, rather than to an addressee. Therefore if anyone posts an item to you at a particular address, we will deliver to that address, regardless of the name on the envelope or package.

I hope you have found this information useful and that this explanation resolves your enquiry and concludes this matter for you. If there is anything else we can help you with though, please contact us again.

Regards

Ian Rutherford
Customer Service Advisor
