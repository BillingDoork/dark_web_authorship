Not to be a dick but this question has been answered and discussed ad nauseam in this section.

You ship a Priority box the same way you ship a Priority envelope.

----Obviously, the box has to fit in the drop box slot.
---- The pkg cannot weigh over 13 ounces.
----Place correct postage on the box. IE: postage stamps. Its best to place exact postage as over stamping is a red-flag.
----All mail dropped in the USPS blue box bearing stamps cannot weigh  over 13 ounces.
----It's best to print out the shipping and return address.
----Try not to use tape on the outside of the pkg..if you must don't use it excessively...another red flag.  *(If you tape over any portion of the stamps the postage is rendered void and will be RTS)
