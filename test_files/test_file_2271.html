Title: Shipping to a house for sale with previous owners name.
Post by: chuk_bery on September 18, 2012, 05:36 pm
Okay so i realize that shipping to a house for sale is generally considered a bad idea but i don't completely buy it. When my buddy was selling his house he would receive mail all the time. Even after someone buys a new home its not uncommon for the buyers to receive mail directed to the previous owners. People on SR seem to not like sending mail to empty house because it would seem "suspicious" but i think this might be exaggerated. Mail men aren't detectives and if the names of the package recipient and owners of the house match up who are they to question the legitimacy of the contents of the package. Sending mail to a house for sale also allows for complete anonymity while other methods involve putting your identity out in the open. Now granted the negative aspect of this method is having to pick up the package at a house that isn't yours but after that is done you would have left no trace for police to find you. Please let me know what you think!
