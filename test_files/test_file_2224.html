Title: Re: Express mail comes with regular mail (USA) ?
Post by: SignalSurfer on September 12, 2012, 01:07 am
It all depends on how quickly your local sorting facility got it and if the package made it to your normal delivery driver in time to go out with the regular mail. I have seen it come many times both ways. Some instances it would come on the regular truck and others a person in a regular (unmarked as far as USPS insignia's) vehicle. The USPS will send it whichever way will get it there by the promised time.

Good luck and I must agree with the above poster. Don't go out and check the mail too often. If something goes wrong and your neighbors see you checking the mail every quarter hour it could be used against you...
