Title: Re: Ordering Weed Internationally
Post by: sinnfein1488 on November 10, 2012, 11:15 am
Depends heavily on what country you are shipping to, and their international mail volume, particularly in regards to the origin country. Some will be scrutinized more than others(orders from south america/netherlands/etc.). The main thing is that in blends in with the other masses of mail and doesn't draw any attention to itself. Of course it needs to be scent free. Double vac sealed preferably. Also, if shipping bud international, that's alot more time to allow for scent to permeate the plastic bags. I feel comfortable doing domestic USA if I receive it within a week or so, any longer than that, I would be concerned with scent.
