Title: Re: Canada -> US Customs?
Post by: slirp on March 28, 2013, 02:13 am
Custom's doesn't decide what they do and don't seize based on the amount they find or what schedule it is.  If they find it then they are going to seize it.  As for personal amounts, realize that your idea of a personal amount and the legal definition of a personal amount are very different.

My experience is that you're better off getting a package from Europe if it goes through east coast customs than you are getting a package from Canada if it goes through west coast customs.  West coast customs is much tougher.  Regardless, the majority of packages from Canada will still make it through.

As always, make sure the drop is clean.
