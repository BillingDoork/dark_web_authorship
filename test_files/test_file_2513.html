Title: Re: Do i have to encrypt my message to buy something?.
Post by: thesearstower on October 21, 2012, 05:10 am
The reason you would want to encrypt your address is this: If you send your address unencrypted, then the SR servers have your order info and address. If LEO controls the server currently, or it is compromised in the future,  there is a chance they've got your info on file. If you encrypt using PGP, then the only person who can read your address is the vendor who posesses the private key. If SR gets busted, all they have is a string of gibberish characters with no way to extract your name or address.

Pretend that your address is a note that you're passing to your vendor through SR. If you just write it out, anyone who wants to look could see it. If you encrypt it, people could still see it, they just wouldn't be able to make sense of it.

Learn GPG. It only takes but a moment, and the peace of mind is priceless.
