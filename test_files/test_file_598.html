Title: Shipping times... delay between order placement and it being sent by the vendor.
Post by: cuppiecake on January 12, 2013, 12:49 pm
I've noticed a lot of vendors quote quite long shipping times internationally and have seen plenty of people speak of 20-40 day wait times.

I had the following experience the past December and I'm curious what comments people have.

It took me about a month (I'm slow) to figure out how to set everything up to place an order, buy bitcoins and get them transferred into my SR wallet and then get comfortable enough to place some orders. In my area (somewhere in Asia) it's almost impossible to get good MDMA (my drug of choice) and I've had a number of bad experiences buying stuff marketed as MDMA but was obviously not to an experienced user.

We had wanted to get some gear for the last new years and in investigating shipping times I was worried that if I only ordered form one Vendor it would not arrive in time. To try and solve this dilemma I decided to place three orders on December 2nd (Sunday - note that I'm ahead of Euro TZ) and I had the following results.

1 order to a vendor based in Germany - extremely well respected SR vendor - order was received on 3 Jan 2013 but was posted on 12 December 2012.
1 order to a vendor based in Europe - extremely well respected SR vendor - order was received on 2 Jan 2013 but was posted on 10 December 2012.
1 order to a vendor based in NL - little known vendor not very active - order was received on 10 Dec 2013 and posted on 3 December 2012.

I was a little annoyed at the NL vendor because their location was not made clear. However, they posted immediately and if I hadn't placed it with them my NYE would have been drug free :(

Now, I'm not complaining as such. We're dealing in a black market after all and all orders made it to me which is the main thing. I'm just curious if others actually pay attention to the date stamps on their packages and if the times people quote are "real" or factor in delays that vendors have in actually posting the product.

Note that I will be ordering from both the first two vendors again for sure. Quality packaging and product. I will not be disclosing names as that's not the purpose of this thread.
