Title: Re: Under auto-finalize it says 15 days
Post by: StonerReakingHavoc on July 08, 2012, 09:05 pm
No it doesn't mean 15 days to arrive, it means 15 days until it auto finalizes. Auto finalize is for sellers protection, say the person orders stuff and gets busted, leaves town or is trying to scam the seller, it will auto finalize on that day to give the seller their money.
When you finalize it gives the seller their money, so only finalize once you get your package. Although some sellers want you to finalize early for new users.
