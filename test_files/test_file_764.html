Post by: MrVidalia on February 24, 2013, 12:23 am
What do you want to hear? The truth is there is no safe amount of any ILLEGAL substance that you can say you could not get in trouble for.

But you can take your precautions, to wit:

Buy from a reputable vendor that is known to have professional packaging.
Have a safe location for a delivery. Not your flat. Not a place where any illegal substance is kept or illegal activities take place ever.
Secure your computer

And then it's pretty reasonable to say that a good majority of your orders in quantities for personal use will not have a problem. And if something does go wrong then there's a good chance there is not enough evidence against you.
