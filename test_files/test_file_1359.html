Title: Re: 2nd delivery to a PO Box.
Post by: SmokesHisBroccoli on July 01, 2013, 04:16 am
I highly doubt there would be any issue.  At least in regards to you not getting any mail there since then.  The issues really only seem to arise if you ordered from a sloppy vendor who sends a stinky package or a suspicious looking package that peaks the curiosity of the postal workers.  But that would happen irregardless of you getting mail or not and is out of your control.  Just sit back, try and relax and I'm sure you'll be receiving your bud in no time without issue.  For the future though I'd try to have mail sent there on the regular.  Not only will it look more legit. but the person in charge of the PO boxes will know your name and there will be less chance of them messing up the delivery to your box.  Do keep in mind people use PO boxes just for delivery of packages because they don't want to risk having it stolen at their residence so there's nothing suspicious about what you're doing.  Rest easy!
