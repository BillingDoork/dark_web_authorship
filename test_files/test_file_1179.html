I know there is always some risk in importing illegal or grey-area drugs from overseas, but I was hoping some people might post who have a better gauge of all this than I currently do.

Lets say I order a kg of methylone.

I know packaging plays a role here, but for the sake of this thread we cant specify that, so lets just say the packaging is "average" - Maybe its bagged in mylar, or just heavt duty plastic, nothing is done to conceal the fact that inside the package is a big back of some chemical, but there is paperwork in chinese claiming it to be some common thing.  This is generally what I have seen in my past orders...
