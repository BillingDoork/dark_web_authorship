Title: Re: Wrong Tracking/Package Siezed?
Post by: Lorimer on July 01, 2013, 08:33 pm
Tracking international orders is a nightmare. I've had tracking tell me an order came through customs, was processed at a sorting facility on one side of the country, then processed at another sorting facility on the opposite coast, then processed AGAIN back at the first, and then it sat in transit for two weeks before finally being delivered - and this was just for a computer accessory. Not all are so bad, of course, but what's happened with your order is not at all unusual. I wouldn't sweat it.

Just try not to obsessively check the tracking.  It won't make it get there any faster.  :D
