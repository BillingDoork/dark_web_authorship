Title: Re: Shipping to college dorm
Post by: asdfsquared on November 19, 2012, 12:54 am
It's not particularly risky. I moved out of the dorms by the time SR came around, so I've never done it, but I don't see much of a difference between shipping to a dorm and shipping to an apartment mailbox.

However, bear in mind that if you're shipping to an address, as long as you keep your trap shut, there's always the plausible deniability of "I don't know why someone else is sending me drugs!" If you've ever gotten an underage drinking ticket in college, though, you'd know that the campus housing 'legal system' isn't bound by the whole 'innocent before proven guilty' thing, and they can and will throw you out of the dorms for even being the type of person that even unknowingly receives drugs in the mail, and possibly out of the university.

It's up to you.
