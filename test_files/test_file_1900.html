Title: Re: Shipping to PO Boxes?
Post by: ftp12 on August 14, 2012, 08:38 pm
Most of the UPS stores and mail places offer private mail boxes. Here they check ID and take a photocopy as well as a second form of ID.  They sign for the package, they hold it in a room behind the boxes until you are ready to pick-up. Go scope them out first, none of them have cameras that I use. Look for neighboring businesses as well. Its good to get boxes where there isn't too much area where LE can set up surveillance. I'm real paranoid so any time I pickup i will drive around the entire area and look for suspicious people/vehicles until I feel comfortable. The mail centers I use are wide open and usually as 1 or 2 dork/high school looking kid working there. Wide open lay out, no back rooms.
