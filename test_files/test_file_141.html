Title: Re: Shipping L domestic
Post by: Wazup7 on November 22, 2012, 02:05 am
Depends a lot on the post.  Light heat and moisture are what cause LSD to degrade.  Light and moisture can be avoided by vac-sealing in a light-proof bag.  So it comes down to heat.

Obviously, the less time in transit, the less time that your goods will be exposed to heat.

That being said, in my experience, it has taken a hell of a lot of heat over a hell of a long period of time to really degrade my blotters.  I had left blotters in my car during the summer (95-100 degrees each day) for about five days, and when I took those tabs, I didn't notice any degradation.  If I had to guess, I would estimate that they could have spent a month in that car and been only slightly degraded.

I wouldn't worry, but if you must, try to purchase domestic if degradation is your concern.
