Title: Canada Customs

Canada customs have been shown on TV some of what they find, how it was shipped and packaged.
This even goes for packages being mailed out of Canada.
Things that they said that they look for are:
- excessive postage
- Target countries know to ship contraband
- They x-ray packages, in and out going.
- wipe down and test for drugs

One package being shipped to China was marked as baby formula, but was found to be cans packed with pot.

The Australian version is a ratings hit and won Most Outstanding Factual Program at the Australian Television Awards in 2009.)
