Title: Re: Pills from NL to UK? (Question for Buyers and Sellers)
Post by: calzone69 on October 19, 2012, 07:00 pm
I am not UK but I am going to give you some generally applicable advice. The customs is not going to x-ray every single parcel that passes them. Usually customs and courier companies x-ray a randomly selected bag of letters and parcels (or bag that a sniff dog has marked) and maybe then it will pass x-ray. Customs usually detect pills in x-ray, believe me, I am from a country with one of the most notorious customs known on SR. It depends on the vendor how well he has hidden the product. If I would ship 100% illegal pharmaceutical pills to someone abroad, I would hide it in a bubble wrap envelope between the paper and the bubble wrap, and put something like an old graphics card in the envelope. The pills would probably show in the x-ray, but when they open the envelope they find the decoy product and they would probably just ignore it. But that's how I would do it. I am not an experience vendor however. Every vendor has his own method of stealth shipping and you should only use reputable vendors with a record of excellent stealth when ordering. When ordering powders like cocaine and heroin vendors should use single or double vacuum seal, when  ordering pharmaceuticals the vendors should be more inventive. That's just my general policy. I guess in UK the customs are not dummies and have all the modern technology at their use.

Oh yeah, the country I am from is Finland. Drug deliveries are intercepted here often and when it comes to pharmaceutical pills customs detect them via x-ray. How is it in UK I am not sure. Not that different I suppose.
