I did receive a sample from Koltbiz and it made it to the US in 26 days.  It does appear that he is having problems getting orders to some places now, but I think he successfully sent out orders for a while, until recently.  I would recommend shipping as a business letter, vs a package.  For the simple fact that it will attract less attention.  Any package coming from Columbia to the US or elsewhere is going to most likely be scrutenized more than many other countries unless they are also drug source countries.
Kolt was talking about shipping from Argentina for a while, but think he dropped that idea.  I would recommend something along those lines as I have heard and tend to believe 'red flag' countries have a harder time getting stuff through than if they weren't on that list.  But, I would still conceal the coke in case the letter/ pkg is opened because if you don't it will be a definite seizure.  Try the business letters first and stick with that starting off.
