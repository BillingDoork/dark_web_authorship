Title: Re: Packaging questions
Post by: tizzat on August 31, 2012, 06:33 pm
if you plan on distributing large quantities I recommend a commercial can sealer, like vegetable can, and a commercial dishwasher, may cook the contents a bit, when you pop the smell is crazy...

For small quantities I recommend USPS First Class. I recommend laying it out flat, then Triple Heat Sealing it - not vacuum, and then wrap in paper towels and printer paper. then put in a thick privacy envelope.. The reason I recommend this is USPS first class super safe and super cheap because its completely operated by machine. the only time first class is handled by hand is when they put it in your mailbox. They are sorted into bags and into bins of thousands, so the police dogs are just overwhelmed and clueless anyway.
