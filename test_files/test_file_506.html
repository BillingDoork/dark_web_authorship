Post by: roachsucker on December 29, 2012, 10:30 am
No experience with US flights, but I have carried drugs (mainly weed, but also others) regularly on flights for many years (intra-EU and international to countries with notoriously strict customs). My 2 cents on the subject are:

- Always keep the dope on you - you may be right that checked-in luggage attracts less attention, but unless you know that for a fact dont assume it. With all the time they keep the check-in luggage they could easily open every single bag, check every single item, and re-package it as it was, without you or i ever being the wiser. I always keep it in either carry-on luggage or a jacket that gets put through the x-ray machine (never on me at all times, in case i get searched after a metal-detector test)

- Dont go crazy with physical concealment - as long as you are carrying personal use amounts (as in your case and in mine) chances of it appearing on an x-ray machine or pretty much nil, and same with a random check. What you're planning to do with the coke is way more stealthy than anything i've ever done

- The biggest problem is sniffer dogs, and no concealment will save you from those, though I'm unsure how sensitive they are... i past by some dogs who were hidden round a corner in germany with a small amount of H and they didnt stop me. On another occasion i was stopped even though i hadnt any dope on me, just by catching the smell from smoking weed the night before, so its variable... i suggest though you wear some clean clothes (i.e. not the clothes in which you were smoking dope all night)

- Also, because of the dogs, I suggest to keep dope always at-hand, so you can *inconspicuously* throw it in a trash can before passing by them (i.e. without having to unpack half your luggage). this is not always possible (sometimes the dogs are well hidden, as was the case in germany where i frankly got lucky), but as last-ditch resort can be necessary... if you're travelling with someone who is aware of what you're carrying they should go in front and act as a spotter for any dogs, but this is best-case scenario...

bottom line is that customs are after traffickers, not people who carry their own stash, and they are trained & equipped to confront traffickers, not users, so there is a very high chance you will be fine... good luck and stay safe
