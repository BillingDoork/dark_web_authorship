Title: Re: Smuggle Liquid content?
Post by: iamonion on September 25, 2012, 05:07 pm
High concentrate in large clear sealed liquor bottle.  Use a syringe to empty and refill with whatever you want.  Inject through top rim cap area of bottle.  Seal with alcohol resistant type clear glue and brush with fine sand paper for a smooth finish.

Airplane, same thing but with bottle size smaller than 2 oz in sealed plastic container.  Don't use acetone.  Simply use Everclear.  Even Everclear is illegal in your state you can purchase it on military installations.  Good luck!
