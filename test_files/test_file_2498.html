Title: Re: Ordering overseas to the US shipping question
Post by: msft1 on June 21, 2012, 04:02 am
I've ordered from NL, Germany, Canada. Only one order from NL didn't show up. However, I also had one domestic order not show up.

It's not so much about WHERE to buy from as WHO and WHAT. Buying easily-shipped goods from any country should be safe. Buying from trusted, reputable vendors who are known for stealthy shipping practice should be safe.

Buying hard-to-conceal (smelly? large?) items OR from newbie vendors who haven't figured shipping out yet should be avoided.
