Post by: firehawk on December 12, 2012, 11:09 am
Quote from: click-bang on December 12, 2012, 02:20 am
is everthing ok ?

Probably.

The greyed out options you describe means your package hasn't been marked in transit yet.

Most likely the vendor can't fill your order right away or hasn't gotten around to it. They're people too, and when real life happens it generally takes priority over sending out SR orders (not saying it's a great business practice, but it's true).

Your bitcoins haven't been released to the vendor yet, so it's unlikely to be any scamming activity.

If you're concerned, write to the vendor and (politely) ask if they need anything else from you to complete the transaction.
