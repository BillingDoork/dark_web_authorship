Post by: captainjojo on August 21, 2011, 09:00 pm
I wouldn't worry about it.  Unless you were expecting a kilo of coke or a couple of pounds of smoke, it probably just got lost in the system or pulled before it reached the US.

I have ordered from the EU several times.  I have been waiting over 6 weeks for a letter that hasn't arrived.  In that time I received a second order from the same seller without any problem.  Two orders, one resend and I only have one letter so far.  I'm disappointed, but not really worried about it.

Either they were taken by some light fingered posties across the pond, they are still on the way, or they were seized but because they are in the EU, they couldn't be bothered to send a letter.

Now, if it had been seized by the USPS you should eventually get a letter saying they have something of yours (usually saying they cannot identify the substance and won't you please come down and help them out).

As stated before, ignore the letter, deny, deny, deny.

The chances of them watching your mail because the intercepted one piece of contraband is pretty low.

So, as I have seen said many times, take a puff, flip on the tube, and relax.

I've been waiting on an order for at least 3 weeks, going on 4 now at least (from over seas). It can take them 4-6 weeks to send a love letter. I have only received one in the last 20 years and I just didn't respond to it.. Unless you ordered something that  is 100 percent legal and they must of made a mistake, then I would leave it alone...

Best advice. Let it go when/if you receive that letter and never sign for anything that would be considered illegal..
