How it works is you propose a date extension, and the seller either accepts it, ignores it, or proposes something else, in which case if they ignore it or propose something else, at the end of the 3 days the resolution admin will step in and decide which proposition will be most likely, in this case since the vendor was vocal about shipping delays, the admin will most likely chose in your favor.
