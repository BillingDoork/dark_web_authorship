Title: Re: PO Box question
Post by: vanilla on August 19, 2011, 07:10 pm
Yes you need 2 forms of ID and one of them must have a photo. The other can be a utility bill or rental agreement etc. You can fake this stuff if you want but you open yourself up to being charged with mail fraud in addition to possession charges if you get busted. The post office does require a warrant in order to open a package tho. It is a trade off and you have to decide what is best for you.
