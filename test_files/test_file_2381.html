Yea man you should be fine, there are several loopholes around it. Remember even if it is a controlled delivery they need to prove that you were aware what the package contained and had Intent. I make it personal habit to just throw the parcel by the door for a few hours incase anything was to go down. I've also heard of people writing on the package "return to sender" and leaving it by the door for the day, which is another very good idea.

Typically I wouldn't worry this much for smaller orders the most you will receive is a letter of seizure, if your ordering pounds of coke/weed then yes a controlled delivery is more likely.
