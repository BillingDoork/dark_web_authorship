Title: Re: LSD, temperatures and domestic shipping
Post by: MissNatural on July 10, 2013, 05:55 am
I've thought about this before, but I don't think most mail sits in an unairconditioned room for very long. I doubt it would ever be left in the back of a truck overnight, and I'm pretty sure the sorting centers would be air conditioned during the night when nobody is there. What I would be most concerned about is the heat of it sitting in your mail box during the time in which it is dropped off. I don't personally know much about how fast LSD breaks down in heat/light, but I think if you just get it from the mail when it gets delivered, then you will be fine.

How to transport LSD privately? Commonly suggested way is to keep it in tin-foil, and then put that in a book, or in the refrigerator hidden or something. It's just a sheet of paper with no odor... can't be too hard to conceal.. be creative.
