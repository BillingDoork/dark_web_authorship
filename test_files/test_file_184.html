Title: Re: Current Best practice BHO shipping
Post by: MendoHashMan on December 12, 2012, 01:00 pm
I got a bunch of glass screw top vials, but if you're only getting a half O at a time, this may not be for you, hell, selling on here might be more then you can handle if that's the quantity you're working with. I bought thousands of jars for cheap since I had about 8 pounds of oil to jar, but you can get them at your local head shop for about 1.25-1.75 a piece, or you can order them from me in the packing material section for a little cheaper. These are a pain in the ass to jar and I have some nerve problems and have to pay people to jar for me, but the jars work great! Easy to pull a dab from, easy to melt out and get every last drop, easily fits in a coin pocket with a paper clip for pulling it out... It all depends on your product, if it's wax or shatter, you can get away with plastic cubes, if it's oil like what I have, these vials are the way to go. If you're just sending one, you can get away with the low postage price on a bubble mailer. If you're sending more than one, pay extra on postage, wrap them tightly in plastic wrap so it doesn't rattle and draw attention, then vac seal in mylar bags to avoid smell and xray detection, if you would like to see an example, just place an order  ;)

Don't use plastic, wax paper is messy but works, glass is the only way to go when it comes to oil imo... Just need to keep it from rattling if you're shipping more then one. Check my listings to see the vials both empty and in use, and place an order to see how they are shipped!
