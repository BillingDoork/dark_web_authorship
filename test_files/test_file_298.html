Title: Re: Who here places orders for their mates and gets delivered to the mates house?
Post by: StrangeHands on October 26, 2012, 06:42 pm
I see no problem with using your account to order for your friends as long as you use their address. They want the drugs, they can take the risk of getting caught. Show them you ordering it for them so there is no doubt that you paid, the danger of it not arriving is also on them.

Do not have it shipped to yourself. Not only are you taking the risk of being caught, but if it does not arrive you have introduced the doubt that you may have just kept it. Having them watch you pay and having it shipped to them lets them know for sure that a non-arrival is the vendors fault not yours.

You should charge a fee for this service, they can after all make their own account and buy their own bitcoins. Another way to go would be to help them set up an account and then sell them coupon codes so they don't have to find bitcoins.

The added bonus is that having multiple names/addresses associated with your account makes it more difficult to profile your account to a specific person.
