There is a lot of discussion on here re the issue you have, in my country the name doesn't matter as it is not linked to an address, I could (and have) used fake names to add to the plausible deniable factor should anything go wrong. Never use a previous occupants name, you have no knowledge re possible redirection instructions that are in place, using your real name though does increase your association to the shipment. My advice would be to invest some time effort and energy into securing a safe drop not associated with you then you thus reducing the risks considerably.
