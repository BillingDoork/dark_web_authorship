Title: Re: Delivery Confirmation w/o going to the post office every time?
Post by: Tryptonite on October 18, 2012, 12:10 am
First, you can not use DCN on first-class letters, only first-class parcels, so do not put on a letter. Also, like 77Tjm said, you can just pick up the DCN's from any post office. They usually have them just sitting out for people to grab, so you don't even have to ask for them. Then you just stick it on the package & add an extra $.75 postage.
