Title: Re: Which substances are most likely to be detected/seized?
Post by: joebiden on July 29, 2013, 04:41 pm
how about this formula for determining the probability of a product being seized (assuming it is illegal):

P(seizure) = 1 - 1/SKETCHY

where SKETCHY = (mass) x (volume) x (how much the product smells) x (1 + number of international borders it will cross)

As SKETCHY approaches infinity, the probability of seizure approaches 100%
