Title: Re: NL to USA - why don't they arrive?
Post by: PotatoConnoisseur on August 26, 2013, 04:23 pm
I see you sell pills, these are a lot more difficult to get through customs than most things. The only advice that I can give is that NL to USA gets looked at a lot closer than most things, just having smell proof packaging won't cut it. If it is opened the customs official needs to see something that makes sense and definitely nothing related to drugs.

For example if you have the pills in smell proof mylar bags then just placed into the package they will probably eventually be found, if a single package gets opened they'll find the pills easily and flag the rest of the packages. If you put the pills in an inner stealth item like a dvd or something the customs official will probably ask himself why somebody in the US orders their dvds from The Netherlands and become suspicious. Then all they have to do is x ray the dvd case or just open it up and once again every package is going to be flagged.

The best stealth is something that makes perfect sense, something legitimate businesses in the Netherlands would sell to people in the United States. So you need to ask yourself what items the Netherlands exports to the US and figure out a way to hide pills in one of these items (or a couple if you want to have multiple stealth options, this way if one gets figured out you don't lose all of your packages) so that the item doesn't look tampered with.

This is all the advice I can give, I've done a fair bit of ordering from Europe but only worked with a couple of vendors in the Netherlands who don't sell pills so I don't have any specific advice (it wouldn't be right to give it out even if I did).
Title: Re: NL to USA - why don't they arrive?
Post by: pharmacypowder on August 26, 2013, 04:42 pm
Firstly my man, how are you shipping your orders? You don't seem to be using any tracking because then it would be VERY easy for you to tell if the package simply has not arrived and is still on its way, or that it is stuck in customs and if it is stuck it customs for a decent amount of time (I have had orders sit in customs over a week and still make it through) consider it lost as it was seized.

So, it seems like you are just shipping by regular mail and if that is the case, have you ever considered the fact that it sometimes will take a lot longer then 15 days to post a letter all the way from the NL to the USA? I am a vendor in Canada that only ships to the USA by regular mail. It is not AT ALL unusual for my orders to take longer then 2 weeks to arrive and I am shipping from the closest country you can get to the USA. In fact, unless you live in the certain close by states, it USUALLY takes close to two weeks....Now, sometimes the mail can be very fast. Sometimes one of my regular customers that usually receives in 10 business days will get it in 4, postal delivery speeds are very inconsistent and I would NEVER ship somewhere so far away like you unless I could track it because chances are, their will be many times that the buyer needs to request an extension on the auto finalize date in the resolution center as it just simply has not arrived because of slow mail.

SECONDLY MAN (AND MAKE SURE YOU REALLY TAKE NOTE OF THIS) You are shipping for THE number ONE "red flagged" country. No matter what country you are shipping too, mail for the NL is scrutinized MUCH more heavily then mail coming in from say England as your country is a known drug source country...Shit, 90% of the time people travel there is because of the drug tourism. Everyone that has spent time on these forums know the ordering from the vendors in the NL have the least likely chance of getting through. Therefore, NL vendors have to have amazing, over the top stealth and pretty much all the big time MDMA/XTC dealers in the NL are famous for their amazing packaging and most of the time their packages will arrive with absolutely no issues.

So, from one vendor to another, my advice to you is only ship that far away if you have a way to track it....That is the only way you will know if it is simply in transit, in customs, or has been delievered just fine and the buyer is just lying to you trying to get a refund and most importantly, you have to have the best of the best stealth. Make is so it can't get any better....Shit, if you have to just place a small order (not from your vendor account obvi) from one of the big guys in the NL that is well known for their packaging so you can get some great idea's from them. Although, since t is a domestic order they most likely won't use the amazing stealth they do to get things across ocean's so perhaps that would not work but if you really think about it and get creative, you'll be able to ship to the USA no problem. If all these other vendors are doing it from the NL then so can you :)
