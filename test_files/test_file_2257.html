I'd bet the previous tenant received your package because most likely, they filed a "change of address" with the PO.  Seeing mail addressed to previous tenants in your box doesn't mean that person isn't getting their mail forwarded.  That's because not all classes of mail are covered by change of address filings and certain classes are only forwarded for 2 months.  Standard length the PO will forward mail is 12 months.  SO, if the mail your receiving in a previous tenants name is either junk mail or bulk rate or magazines only...don't use it.  If you are getting utility bills or personal correspondence in their name then you should be ok.  It's best though, as Mr California suggested...test it out (I'd add do it a couple times by sending yourself  both First Class and/or Priority mail.)
