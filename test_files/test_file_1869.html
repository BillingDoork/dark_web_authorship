A PO box would be safer in terms of not bringing heat to your home, however you should know that they have your name regardless (unless you want to open a fraudulent PO box and get a federal offense when the heat comes down) and would be able to find you. The PO box does have one advantage though, if the seller is a jackass and attempts to search you on FB/google or just threaten to expose you for whatever reason then at least he doesnt have your addy, and if you were smart enough not to give your exact name, that either.

One thing to take note is if you open a PO box and drugs suddenly start coming to it its pretty goddamn obvious you ordered them, whereas a home you can say that you had no clue.

If you don't have a good reason to open a PO box then don't open one.

Good reasons:
-Roommates/parents/whoever will open your packages for whatever reason and freak out when they find your hello kitty acid tabs
-People steal your packages when the mailman is too lazy to leave a notice and simply leaves the package in your mailbox or outside your home.

If you're just a buyer and none of those reasons above apply to you then sending them to your residence is the best way to do it.
