Title: Re: Shipping from NL within EU
Post by: Limetless on August 16, 2012, 11:28 pm
Depending on how much you are buying the NL thing is massively overstated. If you are buying say anything up to 10g of whatever and the product is flat or can be flattened (Blow, Mandy crushed, Cid you get the picture) you basically have next to fuck all to worry about. I have never had a buy stopped from NL and I frequently shop with our friendly Dutch neighbors. If you are talking bulk or weight, yes there is more risk but the Dutch tend to make up for this by having extremely creative shipping, much more than people from less high-profile countries. So really NL is more of a risk yes but they balance it out by putting in much more effort when it comes to stealth.

Hope this answers your questions. :)
