Title: Re: Flattened nugs?
Post by: mcguire39 on June 27, 2013, 11:49 pm
Generally people don't want flattened nugs. Some people use a box; others a padded envelope. Both seem to work as long as the innermost bag or two is just sealed (but not vacuum sealed). Make sure to read up on mylar and MBB's; just the plain clear polyethylene food sealer bags alone are not adequate, they will scent through pretty easily. The mylar, MBB's, foil seem to work extremely well.
