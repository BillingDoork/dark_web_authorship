Honestly the best idea for this is to go to the largest church in your local area and get a church directory.  This will allow you access to one of the larger local databases of elderly individuals.  Eliminate all elderly who look to be physically fit, have a glimmer of liveliness in their eye, or people that you know to be active in the community.  You can then create a map of your local area of these houses.  If you spend a little time following the mail carriers (careful with this, don't want to appear suspicious) you can get a good idea of what time mail is delivered on a certain route.  Honestly if your business is successful enough you can just buy gps trackers and plant them on the mail vehicle in question.  You rotate the addresses and carrier routes and you could receive quite a few packages this way (assuming they are not too large to fit in a mailbox).
