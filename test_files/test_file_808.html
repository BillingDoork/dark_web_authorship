Post by: Cali4niaLove on March 07, 2013, 04:33 am
Did you open your PO Box at at United States Post Office?

I would suggest opening it somewhere else (a mom and pop PO Box center would be ideal)

As for using a fake business name, go through the local process to register a business using your real information (should cost you less than $100)....then you will have paperwork to go with whatever business you register....scan that paperwork, open up photoshop and change whatever details you want. Now you have a fake business with legit paperwork to open a PO Box.

As for questions regarding specific PO Box Center practices, utilize good ol fashioned social engineering, come up with a backstory to tell them and then ask questions that are innocent but will still reveal the information you are looking for.

As for how you pick up the package, you always want to draw the least amount of attention to yourself. Sunglasses and a hoodie aren't a good idea. Besides, when you opened your box they made a copy of your ID anyway didn't they? They already have your face on file.

If you are super paranoid and can convince a friend to work with you, have a friend register the box using a fake ID, then you can be the one that picks up packages. If you don't look alike, this will present a dead-end for LE conducting an investigation.

Post by: Drippy on March 07, 2013, 08:25 am
For what it's worth, I've gotten the slip in my box twice now to come sign for it when I know I didn't have to.  In the end, I decided to just man up and go up to the counter.  Post offices fuck up a lot, and I wouldn't be surprised if it was a mistake like it has been twice for me now.

As far as plausible deniability, you're throwing that out the window either way.  Taking a package from your box and walking out is just the same as signing for it at the counter, so I wouldn't factor that into your decision.  If it were them leaving a note at your door to come pick up a package they wouldn't deliver that'd be a completely different story, but it's not.

My opinion is that you just go get it.  Unless it's LSD crystal or some nbome substance, LE isn't going to give a flying fuck about 5g.  They would've just sent you a love letter, not stationed an officer at the post office to wait for days on end for you to come take ownership of <$1000 worth of illicit substances.
