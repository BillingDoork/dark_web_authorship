Post by: BabyBananaGrabber on August 20, 2013, 02:49 am
I don't. For one thing, I research the vendors I use, and when I buy from someone, I'm confident (or at least as confident as it's possible to be) that they're good about stealth. More importantly, I'm too small-time to be worth the effort of busting. Even assuming LE were to find out I ordered drugs (and taking just a few basic, common sense precautions means they almost certainly won't), it's highly unlikely they'll consider the effort and expense of prosecuting me for my 2 pot brownies or 10-strip of acid to be worth it--I'd be far more likely to get a love letter; and while that would suck, it just means that I have to start over with a new place to receive my orders. And even if everything does go wrong and LE does show up at my door, I've still got plausible deniability.

Now, obviously this may not apply to those who do order large amounts of drugs, or to other countries. But for me, I don't worry about the period when a shipment is in transit. Besides, even if there were a reason to be nervous, once the package is in transit, there's not much I can do about it anyway--so why stress out about it?
