Post by: princeblack49 on October 01, 2013, 04:16 pm
These are my self governed rules on receiving

Rule one: Check the vendors stats. Lots of auto-finalize could mean lots of controlled delivery (maybe-just a theory of mine) Generally buyers will say if it came in good order and I never order from a bad stealth review vendor even if I really really want it. MBB package always on stuff dogs are trained to sniff out. I have gotten plenty of vacuum sealed stuff but the data is in on this question as researched by SR users who own a drug dog.

Rule two. NO PRIVNOTE ALWAYS USE PGP. Trust Edward Snowden on this - nuff said here.

Rule three: I never use a friend to receive packages. I have heard too many stories about how a controlled delivery scares the receiver into admission and they will be an accessory to your activity. Keep all your business to yourself the there are no questions about how things went wrong if they do and nobody else to drag down with you if it goes awry.

Rule four: Do not use a PO Box. I use a private mailbox/shipping company with night access for larger packages and my home for first class envelopes. Real name and ask sender to make everything look like normal mail with a plausible return addy and not overtaped. No attention drawn = no issues. If it looks odd it is not stealthy.

If you read the OPs in the stickys in security and shipping and use PGP (if you have an issue with PGP just wait until you fix it to order. You can get help on the forums)

Some say wait a day to open a package. Research plausible deniability and know the rules.
