Go to any busy Post Office that services a large number of box rentals.  Most of them have a table or two where people often stop and go through the mail they've just picked up.  At thew end of these tables are usually large trash cans where people discard unwanted mail.  You'll find tons of other people's junk mail as well as magazines/periodicals, retail catalogs, etc.  Inside most of these magazines you'll find a tear out form used to subscribe.
