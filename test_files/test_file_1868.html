Post by: quinone on June 04, 2012, 11:21 am
You will NOT have to sign for lettermail shipped express from Canada to the USA.

Further, many get confused cuz the name of Canada's 'basic' first trackable method to the US is called Xpresspost, and that's just the name of the mailer/service .... it's not actually express delivery.  So are you sure the vendor's shipping didn't say US -> Xpresspost or Expresspost (in one word)?  Cuz that's a brand, not a service.

Oh and too add, it cost extra for the canadian shipper to buy signature at request, so unless your vendor is the dopiest character around, he didn't explicitly go and spend extra money to make sure your package needs to be signed for.
