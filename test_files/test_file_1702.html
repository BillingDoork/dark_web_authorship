Title: Re: good stealth tips?
Post by: dipset on September 12, 2013, 09:28 am
In my opinion, most of the time proper exterior shipping methods like proper postage, return addresses/individual name or business being legitimate and not standing out, and the correct choice of mailers without excessive taping, uneven weight distribution, small as possible, flat as possible (if envelope), no stuff rattling around, etc. will get you through no problem. Domestic anyway. The person doing the drop if it isn't in a blue box, must be on the level as well and not get nervous, or profiled somehow. The inside of the pack should at least have several layers of whatever (I prefer a box inside another box, for bigger things) or another envelope if that is the mailer type being used. Many opaque layers...

Obviously odor containing sealing materials are a given amongst that. But if you really want to be "stealth," then hide the impeccably multiple layered sealed item inside a legit product and/or box with the rest of the parcel (return label, packaging, etc.) supporting the facade of some retail item that could be sent by any number of legit online retailers or an ebay/amazon type situation.
