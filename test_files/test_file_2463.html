Title: Re: Was this a Controlled Delivery of some kind? Advice NEEDED!!
Post by: BlarghRawr on October 13, 2012, 07:48 am
They'll attempt another delivery if you don't come by the post office to pick up the package. At that time, you'll be able to see whether or not the signature is waived and if it is you can just point that out to the postman. And if it isn't waived, you have rights to a partial-to-full refund on the grounds that the vendor was supposed to waive it and claimed he did, thus it was either compromised(Thus, vendors fault for packaging that couldn't make it past inspection) or the vendor lied/fucked up/something, in which case it is still the vendor's fault for not waiving it.
