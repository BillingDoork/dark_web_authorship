Title: Receiving bulk orders
Post by: 46andtwo on May 20, 2013, 01:40 pm
Alright so I may be making my first bulk order (or maybe not, who knows?) and I'm curious on finding info on receiving bulk orders. I've been searching the shipping and security forums, but haven't seen any specific topic on receiving bulk orders. I've come across a few ideas, 'drop-off's', friend's houses, abandoned houses (no) and such but none of these appeal to me. Is there a time proven safe and effective method to get your bulk orders in? Would it make sense to ask the vendor to break the order into a multiple shipments? What's the best way to go about this?
Title: Re: Receiving bulk orders
Post by: entreterra on May 20, 2013, 02:01 pm
You can always ask the vendor if he/she would split the order into multiple smaller orders for a fee (for the extra cost in shipping and the trouble).

If you don't have a friend who would voluntarily allow it to be shipped to his/her house, then have it delivered to your name and address; not a good idea to send to an unaware neighbor, abandoned house or fake name etc.

If you are shipping a bulk order domestic, there really shouldn't be anymore concern than a small order. If it's an international bulk order, then relocate all drugs and drug parphenellia to another location (friend's house) as well as the computer you used to order (unless you're running tails or encryption software [truecrypt], but still may be good to have absolutely nothing at your residence where the order is being delievered to). Keep a totally clean house until the package arrives and to be safe for several days after you get it too.

So long as you have not had international packages go missing in the past en route to your address, you should be good!

Order from a vendor with reputable stealth and high delivery success.

best of luck to you!
