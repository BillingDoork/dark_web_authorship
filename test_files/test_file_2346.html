Post by: nef on September 30, 2012, 08:13 am
Radiodog, what you saw was nothing out of the ordinary.  Mail carriers carry all of the letter-sized mail in their first route walk.  If it's a heavy mail day, and they didn't have room for all the packages in their bag, then a few minutes later, they'll do a separate round with packages, sometimes even more than one if there are big packages.

Obviously, this doesn't apply if your carrier is a driver, like in a rural area.  Another possibility is that there was a misdelivery, and one of your neighbors got the package and put it in the correct box.
