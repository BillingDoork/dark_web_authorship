Title: Re: Shipping Edibles
Post by: monrovia on December 10, 2012, 04:54 am
If there's THC in it, it's definitely going to smell like weed to a dog. Hell, humans can usually smell the bud in edibles if you put your nose close enough to it, but they still can't get dogs to sniff out every package that goes through customs, far from it most likely. If you order from a reputable seller though, I'm sure they will take appropriate precautions as far as packaging goes. Check their feedback and search for them in the forum. I would guess that things can get a little weird when dealing with edibles as oppose to raw cannabis depending on where you are, but even if shit does hit the fan, the worst thing that will probably happen is you getting a letter from customs saying that what was being shipped to you was illegal.
