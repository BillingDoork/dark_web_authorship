Title: Re: drop house
Post by: PowerToCharm on July 28, 2013, 04:21 am
Filling out a forwarding form with the postal service will have no effect whatsoever on UPS or Fed Ex, as they are all totally independent of one another. Of course most normal (?) people submit a change of address to the postal service when they move, but the change of address is only for certain people, not just any mail for anyone that comes to the house. Otherwise, if you moved and someone else started living in your house, all their mail would get forwarded to you. Also, I don't know why having a for sale sign in the yard should necessarily keep the mail from getting delivered, as lots of people live in their houses while they're for sale.
