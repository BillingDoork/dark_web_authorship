Tracking will not tell you if you're shipment was seized, it just won't update and eventually the tracking number will stop working after awhile. If for some reason your shipment is seized, hopefully they'll send you a letter to notify you about it so you can at least know what happened to it. They used to send out love letters a lot but nowadays you won't always get a seizure notice.

BUT I have had a few packages get stuck at ISC for even longer then 14 days so don't give up hope yet.
