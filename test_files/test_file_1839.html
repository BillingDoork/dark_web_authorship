Post by: gracevan3569 on July 21, 2012, 05:19 am
First and most importantly, if you STAY IN ESCROW, you will eventually be able to get your btc back.  You will eventually enter resolution and make your claim. If the product does not arrive and the vendor does not have proof of it being sent/delivered you will be good to go.  It may take time to get to that point.

A lot of things can happen on the vendor side to cause him to not get right back to you, but if it has been a week that is odd.  Have other buyrs left very recent feedback on his page? Are they good?  Any updates on his page?

I'm sure others can also offer advice, but do not finalize.  That is your only protection.

Also, if you are bran new, why go jumping into a large buy?  This is one of the biggest problems for new folks.  Get to know a vendor with small buys and communication.  Being eager will get you scammed.
