I don't know what the laws are like in your area, but all I can say is ordering a personal amount your first time will definitely minimise any risk of an arrest. You're more likely to have your package confiscated than any follow up investigation. Do your research for your local area though. And if the worst should happen have a plan in place. Read up on how people suggest you deal with LE to get more detail, but the general practice is to say nothing to them until you have spoken to a lawyer.
