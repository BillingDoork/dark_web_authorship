There's a very large online auction site called eBay: you might have heard of it.  Postal workers send millions of packages every day which were bought and sold on eBay.  There are many people who use their eBay sales for a supplemental income and a few who even earn a living working from home.  If your packages look and smell like typical eBay packages you should have no worries.

IOW, don't overthink this stuff.  Vacuum seal where appropriate and make your package look like something somebody might have ordered from a trinket vendor and you're golden.  At the end of the day, you're just another small online businessperson.
