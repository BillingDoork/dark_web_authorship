Express mail is TYPICALLY a separate truck. Also, it's either BY 12pm or BY 3pm depending on where you live.

Title: Re: usps express shipp.
Post by: Aidoneus on October 16, 2012, 04:44 am
We ship a fair amount of overnight packages... but I suppose mileage can vary.

Why exactly are you going to be 'shitting bricks'? Domestic packages have virtually a 0% chance of failed delivery... unless the vendor is a fucktard.

As far as your question - depends on what the seller did. Did they check the box to 'Waive Signature'? If so, the mail guy will stick it in your box if it fits. Or they may leave a notice saying you need to go pick it up at the post office. If the vendor didn't waive the signature... you will have to sign for it (surprise, surprise).
