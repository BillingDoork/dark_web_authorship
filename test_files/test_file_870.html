I have been here for 4 months with 30 transactions and I can tell you from experience: it's Canada Post dude. They are the most useless unhelpful organization on the face of the Earth. As a fellow Canuck I strongly recommend that you request all future orders to be sent as REGISTERED MAIL: meaning that all future deliveries are confirmed by your signature. The Domestic Charge is $1.50 and for American purchases it's an additional $10. bucks... which for me is well worth the peace of mind.This will ensure that you either get the package or have recourse for replacement/refund if you don't and won't have to get all paranoid about imagined LE interception.

I imagine that many of our American SR users might be horrified by my advice to sign for a package containing illegal drugs... and if I were an American I certainly wouldn't sign for any 'iffy' package either... but remember: Canada does not have the same aggressive 'War on Drugs' infrastructure (DEA/Dept.Homeland Sec/U.S Customs) or harsh penalties that the U.S. has......
