Title: Re: Package Seized- How do I tell from Tracking
Post by: WhiteShark on March 19, 2013, 05:44 pm
I agree with th3creeper, however don't read too much into it. As a seller I have seen packages go through exactly what you are describing, on several cases. In every single case, the package was successfully delivered (despite an initial scare that this one was going to be my first seized package).

To specifically address your concerns:

1. The item on hold --> the postal office/outlet is dealing with a high volume of packages. Why does yours go on hold? Cause you need to wait your turn in line. But they update the tracking just so they know the last location it was. It is not at the delivery office, so should be going out soon

2. Customs not showing on tracking --> while I can't guarantee my explanation is correct, I have seen it happen before and get delivered. My explanation, either the package seems fair enough/stealth enough that it just passes through customs so it is not scanned. Or they just forget to scan it all together at customs.

So do not worry mate, I have been in your shoes, only to see it get Successfully Delivered after 2-4 days. Contact the seller, see what they say, and just wait it out for a week or so. If after a week nothing shows, then maybe it is time to panic!

But I would keep your house clean just in case, although in Canada to get a  house warrant off a single drug package with no other evidence would be next to impossible. Unless you have a record of trafficking or something similar, I just don't see your house getting raided off a single package. Most likely love letter, but lets hope its just delayed. Keep us updated!
