You should use some of the bags that you seal up with an iron.  I think they are 225 or 300 micron bags?  someone is selling them on here for a reasonable price.  yes, definitely triple vaccum seal, and no definitely do NOT use a priority envelope.  are you crazy?  a half pound in a priority envelope is going to stick out like a sore thumb.  It will probably be way thicker than 3 inches not to mention the corners of a priority envelope are nothing close to airtight so if you're concerned about smell this would be a major issue.

User a flat rate BOX to ship them, and if anything, given your talking about moving a pound in two shipments, a better idea would be to SWARM each HP by breaking it down two 4 shipments of individual ounces through priority, 4 ounces shipped individually for each half pound.  That way if one package gets seized you stand to lose 1/4 of the weight for each half pound vs. losing the whole thing. This is how real deal smugglers move shit through the mail when they make bulky moves.

If everything is going to the same address then swarming it like that is could look sketchy and pop a flag or 6, hah, but I'd still recommend at least breaking into two shipments for each half pound.  better yet, have the friends you're shipping to give you a few addresses so they can each receive a piece of the whole.  Then you can do the originally proposed method of four shipments per HP, so four 1oz shipments.

Ship each of the four on different days for each HP.  I would recommend doing 2 1 week and 2 the next.  That's my .02.  good luck with everything
