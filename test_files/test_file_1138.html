Title: Re: Shipping product without altering/changing shape or form
Post by: AfternoonDelight on May 20, 2013, 04:03 am
Everyone wants stuff that's sold as close to the source as possible, with the best potency, flavour and appearance.
The picture is usually representative of what you will get.  If I see a pic of a block of coke and I'm ordering a gram, I don't expect a chunk of coke though, I expect some finely ground powder.
Some people have had problems in the past with "crushed bud" here on the road.  Generally if the product is decent and makes it to the customer, SR is on the vendors side.  I've never seen SR step in to help one of those crushed bud guys.

Appearance I think should make the difference between a good vendor and a great vendor, but I think appearance alone should not dictate if someone is a bad vendor or not - unless it appears someone layed a big duke on your order before they packaged it.

That being said, I think the pic should be representative of what you get, and if they don't have one then they should leave it blank instead of stealing a mis-representative pic elsewhere.
