Post by: geeza23 on December 16, 2012, 09:32 am
welcome to the forums, but there's probably hundreds of threads either asking this or addressing what you're asking right now so please search in the future.

In regards to what you're asking, you probably shouldn't start off with ordering 10 or 20 grams once off. Make smaller orders of 2-4? Depending on what you're comfortable with, also drug laws really depend on where you live as they differ accordingly e.g. some countries .1g and you get the death penalty whereas some countries decriminilise up to 7g. Also do tons of research on vendors and the general consensus is ordering domestic will almost guarantee that you'll get your package since it doesn't have to go through customs.

If you've done all your research and take the necessary precautions, the chances you will end up being busted and ruining your life will be extremely low (bear in mind there is still a slight chance of things going wrong, you are after all ordering drugs in your mail).

Finally, if you do find yourself facing a LEO, the most important thing is to not tell them anything at all, and just ask for a lawyer. Once again, if you've followed all the steps necessary, and didn't do anything stupid like ordering a kilo of weed, chances are it'll be extremely hard for them to prove anything that will seriously be against your favor.

All the best.
