Tablet presses area actually very easy to get, even the multi-die rotary machines that press out 12,000+ tabs an hour depending on the model. We have tablet presses as well as mixing machines. Anyway, what questions do you have? The filler typically used is cellulose and as far as die goes, you can just use the primary RGB colors to make them turn out the way you want. Also there are many things that factor the color of the tablet even adjusting the pressure will change the color of the tablet. It's recommended to not user more than 40% active ingredient and the rest filler. Meaning if you have a 250mg tablet, the most you could use is 100mg of whatever it is you are pressing. You play around enoungh with the machines you will figure it out pretty easy.
