Title: Re: UK siezures ?
Post by: RootZero on August 05, 2012, 09:29 am
I can only speak from my experience so take it with a pinch of salt. :-*

I think posties get a bad wrap. I see mine every day and he's a nice chap, I'm a good judge of character (I like to think! :P) and he wouldn't steal a thing, he's the family type. Poor chap just has to do an epic delivery route with a huge bag to lug around for probably minimum wage.

Maybe the sorting offices are where dodgy things go on? When they do an x-ray analysis they do it on a pallet of mail at a time and spotting a small amount is hard, but not impossible. Using sniffer dogs is impractical and never heard of them being used. I don't actually know how much UK domestic mail actually gets scanned or scrutinized.

I would guess they mainly focus on international mail, I also guess they find a lot of contraband all the time in small quantities, MOM isn't new, the easiest explanation is it just gets destroyed, there's be little point following up on it for a small quantity.
Also weed is probably hardest to hide of all drugs, pre-ground, vacuum sealed in a thin envelope has been very successful for me, even for high risk countries like The Netherlands and USA (not through SR tho). But

Also I think the Unions or the RM have changed all their delivery routes and drops slightly in some places due to health and safety nonsense about postmen doing their backs in and getting injured (which doesn't surprise me!). I'm sure I got a leaflet through about it a few months back.

Also the Olympics, there must be increased security, especially for London, unless they are stupid, its the only reason this would delay things no matter how much they harp on about 'logistics'. Blame it the Olympics, it's everyone's RL excuse these days! :P

Here's their clearnet statements for personal mail currently:
http://www.royalmail.com/2012/personal

Should probably have a careful look round the packaging posts, find international / domestic often get mixed up on there but there's probably much more info somewhere on this. Limitless would know.  ;)

Royal Mail are definitely slow recently and getting worse, first class sent in the morning used to be next day 90% of the time. But it gets there in the end for 99.9% of the time. This is getting slower all the time for me tho. Birthday cards are a mare recently! >:( Used to know they would turn up on the day.

If your ordering in the UK mail is pretty much guaranteed to get there eventually. If a domestic small order doesn't get there, keep a close eye on the vendor. [I'm sure people will disagree with me tho].
For international airmail, more like 80-90% is my experience if the packaging is pro.

I know people obsess about receiving pretty un-flattened buds, but I don't mind pre-ground, so long as its decent bud.

Without revealing too much, decent printed labels imitating a professional company address stickers, vacuum sealing, pre-ground is best and can even be sent in a window envelope imitating a letter. Some examples of packaging I've had are fairly bad in some cases.
