Title: Re: Recent Order- had writing on package
Post by: firehawk on December 14, 2012, 07:28 am
Sounds like you dodged a bullet.

I can't think of any practical SUS acronyms that would fit the signature requirements of a mail article... Signature Upon S...something is about as close as I can get. That, and the fact that what was written on the package "Signature not required" is in fact--in my opinion--a little suspicious, leaves me thinking that a mail clerk noticed it and marked it as suspicious. Judging from it arriving unmolested, it seems that it was either not followed up or when it was checked over nothing was found. Given that a warrant is required before USPS staff can open mail packages, they may have just not had the resources to give it a proper inspection.

I think you should let your vendor know about this. Even if it was something else, the less attention any package draws the better.
