Title: Re: Odor Packing Advise
Post by: macdrizzle on October 03, 2012, 07:51 am
vacuum seal. x2 or x3.
scrub the outside of the vac sealed bag with ammonia or bleach or at least alcohol swabs to get any drug residue scent off of it.
wear new gloves after handling the drugs and sealing up the first vac seal. Wear new gloves at each step. Don't contaminate the outside of the package or any part with drug residue.
Dont' smoke weed during packaging. Don't expose the package/envelope to MJ smoke.
Don't worry about putting odor or cover smells in the package. It's not useful when a dog is bing used to detect drugs/cash. That will only fool humans who get overwhelmed by the more powerful scent in a mix of smelly things. Dogs just smell everything (drugs + the decoy/cover odor), and if you put the wrong thing in, it may ATTRACT the dog to your package.
