It's better than getting the police involved.

I'm 99% sure that I won't get any of the heat since my prints are not on the package but I can't say the same for my supplier. I appreciate the sellers on here and wouldn't want them to get busted.

Maybe OP was partially responsible for this, but in his plight there is a real message to be headed. Most vendor's packaging is far beyond terrible, most packages could be opened and known instantly, a little real stealth would be awesome.

Stealth means the return address matches the contents, and is a real legitimate address.
Stealth means vendors fingerprints shouldn't be all over the inside or outside of the package.
