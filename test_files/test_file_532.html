go to a pay phone and ring up asking where it is.  It's not unusual for someone to be tracking down a package.  If they ask for your name give them another name say your sending  it to a family member as a birthday present and its late or something.  I have done this before when tracking  down SR orders.
From my research if they tell you that they dont know where it is then it has been seized but since it says forward for delivery you should be ok.  With new years and public holidays just wait another couple of days.
