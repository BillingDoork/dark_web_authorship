Title: Re: Processed Through Sort Facility: ISC SAN FRANCISCO (USPS)
Post by: oxyhoot on February 06, 2013, 09:14 pm
I once had a package stuck at the NY ISC for 10 full days. I was beginning to worry and then on day 11 it showed that it had departed to my local sort facility and I had it on day 12. The packaging was completely non-stealth which leads me to believe the next paragraph.

Just out of curiosity, what country is it coming from? I understand if you don't want to answer but for example there is very little US customs checking for packages coming from other English speaking countries, including India. If it's coming from the Netherlands, you can expect it to be checked pretty thoroughly. Also, there is almost no customs checking on letter mail unless it is obvious it contains objects. I have gotten letter mail from the EU in 4 days before because they were packaged well.
