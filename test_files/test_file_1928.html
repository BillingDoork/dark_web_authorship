Title: Re: Best way to buy Airmail Stamps in the UK?
Post by: PlutoPete on August 25, 2012, 04:05 pm
if you do it through their website you can be traced.
Best thing is to just buy a bunch of stamps and pick up a bunch of airmail stickers from the post office, get your customs stickers here too.
Get a pamphlet with the weights and prices and international zones in it, it's number is rmop1
Weigh your parcel, affix suitable stamps according to weight and zone, make up bullshit for the customs sticker, and away you go :)
up to 100g it's £2.70 europe and £3.30 rest of the world, but for heavier items there are more regions.
