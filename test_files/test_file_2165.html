Ima NOOB here at SR, But by no means a NOOB to shipping substances via USPS..Id like to ad my expertise...I am sure everyone takes precautions by vac sealing etc..
1- I have found that Turkey roasting/basting  bags are far superior to use for shipping MJ...
2- USPS can and does use a probe to pierce packages(with no search waarrant} to check contents by whats on probe.The best bet is to conceal your package in a "probe proof" container..Something hard like a metal cookie tin or HARDPLASTIC TUPPERWARE or other NON PIERCEABLE container.

Over 17 yrs using USPS and have NEVER lost a package that wasnt put in a hard no pierceable container..IMAO THIS IS A MUST...Bu do as you please, just offering my advice from decades of using USPS....
