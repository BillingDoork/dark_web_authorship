Here is the explination on stats from the wiki (http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Feedback_Score)

In order for a vendor's rating to appear next to their name, they must be reviewed enough for it to be a meaningful average. This requires roughly 10 reviews per month, but can be more or less depending of the weights of the reviews.


From a few things I read here, it is a very good idea to make the return address in the same postal code as where you are making the drop.  It has been suggested by some that packages with postal codes different from where they were collected could be looked at more closely.  Add to this the fact that every single piece of mail is photographed before it is processed could help with identifying abnormalities such as this
