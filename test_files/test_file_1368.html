Look into the Dymo label printers... they use thermal heat on their labels to print, so none of those 'microdots'

Eitherway, you should be careful if you are shipping out of the house where you receive your orders... if that's the case, never have any vending supplies inside the house when receiving the orders, or ever if you can help it. Be creative and figure something out. If they're gonna bust you vending anyways I seriously doubt they're going to do it via analyzing the dots on your printer and tracking you down... Most vendors who get busted get busted for something IRL related or perhaps they get raided due to some faulty delivery and have vending shit in their house. Just some ideas to think about.
