Title: Re: would ordering from the US to the UK really be a good idea?
Post by: CaptainSensible on July 17, 2013, 07:26 pm
My understanding, from reading the messages on this forum for a couple of years, is that all packages sent from one country to another are much more likely to be flagged for inspection. It's always tempting to order from another country when you're looking for someting special that is not available locally. Be careful though, and be aware that you're more likely to never get your order from overseas. Domestic shipments, in most countries, are far safer.
