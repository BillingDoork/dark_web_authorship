Title: USPS Domestic to PO box: Sketchy. "Notice Left". Comments/Advice?
Post by: qulilluzm2 on November 15, 2012, 05:23 pm
Hello everyone.
Trying to stay optimistic here but so many sketchy things have been happening with one package I ordered from a 100% vender with 200+ transactions and very happy customers. Had one experience before with this vendor and the packaging is as good or even better than the other handful of vendors I have used.
I just can't hold back not to post anymore because USPS seems to be very sketchy with this package. It's making me nervous.
First off the package was sent late Oct right before a certain "super storm". I suppose due to this, the package was delayed a good amount of time. The vendor saw that I didn't get the package yet to they handed me the DCN about a week after shipment.
We saw it was stuck in a sorting facility near him. By doing some research I found (assume) that the sorting facilities were holding back packages due to the storm until PO re-opened.
A couple days ago I got happy because the package moved to around my area. Then finally a day later moved to my town.
Then it got really sketchy.
First it said "Out for Delivery"
(Checked, but no delivery was made the whole day)
Then 14 hours later (automatically) it said "Delivery Status Not Updated"
It said this for a while. Then finally after this in the morning it said "Notice Left". Funny thing is too that when it said "Notice Left" the stamped time date was updated in reality at 11:30am but online it says the marked time was 9:30 if you know what I mean.
Under the tracking number it says this:
"www.usps.com/redelivery or calling 800-ASK-USPS, or may pick up the item at the Post Office indicated on the notice. If this item is unclaimed after 30 days then it will be returned to the sender. Information, if available, is updated periodically throughout the day. Please check again later."
So far in total from shipment of seller its been over 3 weeks (But remember that storm definitely had to delay things)

ANYONE have an experience like this? Still didn't check my box today but probably will at some point to see whats really in there.
All I know is I know nothing!  :-X

But really any advice is appreciated. If there really is a notice for me to get it think I'm just gonna call the game to an end for me unfortunately. Still can't see how this vendors packaging messed up, but I'm also thinking it could be that a PO worker thinks I did something strange or flagged my box.
Thinking the worst but hoping the best!

EDIT/UPDATE:
Everything went just fine. Thank you all for the comments and reassurance! For future reference to others I will lock the topic and say what happened. I think it was an automatic clock punch that made it say "Notice left". I think that they had a backup of deliveries and couldn't deliver on the date they should of. Therefore, the tracking automatically updated to notice left but in reality no notice was left. It was just delivered the next day. No problems.  ;D
