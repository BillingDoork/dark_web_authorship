Title: Re: A lot of my orders don't arrive, Germans please advise
Post by: heatcheck on May 24, 2013, 05:38 pm
I've ordered multiple things at once and it has been okay. But I have been advised against it on these forums.

Look at it this way, if package A is intercepted by the police.... and packages B, C and D are still out there.... chances are they are going to be grabbed also. Now you have four illicit packages coming to you.

If you order just one at a time, then if one is grabbed thats it. And you can move on with another address.

Sorry I didn't address your actual concern. But I'm not German. But I think situation above is one to consider.
