Post by: nanpa2001 on January 12, 2013, 01:55 am
goochihuh,

SR gives vendors 4 days to process an order. When you put your pin number in and make the purchase, it is a done deal. You do have the option of canceling the order if the vendor does not ship in a 'timely' manner. 'Timely' is considered up to 4 days. After that an option to cancel the order appears.

None of this has anything to do with a vendor not answering your messages.
