I didn't visit your link, but do yourself a favor and just work your way through the guides on these forums to get the proper software. It's really easy. Since i'm bored, I'll run down what i did with my first order.

-Make sure your name and address are exactly as it should appear on the package shipping label. Vendor will copy / paste it. Encrypt name and address with vendor's public key. Copy resulting outputted code and paste into SR order address box. Place order.
-Your vendor will receive the encrypted address message and use his public key to decrypt it. Then he'll copy/paste the clear text to a label, print, and stick it on your package.

-Send your public key to the vendor so he can also send you encrypted messages that only you can read. You could just send your pub key  to him unencrypted I suppose. I was paranoid at the time and used the vendor's public key to encrypt a message containing my public key. Probably unnecessary. Anyway, send it to him in a PM, not the order address window.

-From then on whatever sensitive info you send him you would encrypt with HIS public key. Whatever you receive from him you decrypt with YOUR private key to read it.

Make sense?  No?  Eh... just read the guides. lol.
