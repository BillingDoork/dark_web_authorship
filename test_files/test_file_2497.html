Title: Re: how much weed can you get before it being suspicious?
Post by: 77Tjm on October 17, 2012, 07:27 pm
Buy random odds and ends from ebay (small cheap electronics accessories eg cell phone covers or extra cell phone chargers work well) at the same time as the other purchases to add a legitimate mail screen. Beyond that, use reputable sellers (read feedback and look for comments about sloppy shipping), make sure your address is safe to deliver to (colleges, shared houses, apartments have more opportunity for theft/discovery/mis-delivery), and don't do anything stupid like using a fake name. Good Luck!
