Title: Re: Most versatile label printer?
Post by: maxhavelaar on August 25, 2012, 09:47 pm
labelprinters are very expensive, here locally in the Netherlands at the action store (a european discount chain) you can get 15 sheets A4 size with 24 labels each for €0.98 (360 labels). A comparable roll for a labelprinter costs €19 at the office supply store here, for 220 labels! DYMO labelprinters cost another €80,-, for which you can get a new inkjet printer as well.

An inkjet printer can print directly to envelopes as well, and it's easy to use a program like coreldraw to put the addresses in the right spots for an a4 sheet. You can even use a sheet more than once. basically one label off a roll for a DYMO labelprinter is more expensive than a whole A4 sized sheet with 24 labels. The only benefit is that you get a label at a time.
