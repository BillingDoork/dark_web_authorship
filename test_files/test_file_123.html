Post by: CBUKBOOK on September 13, 2013, 12:47 pm
I'm from Israel and i have made 4 purchases from SR.
My own personal rules are these:
1. Never buy anything that doesn't fit a small letter or parcel so i never have to worry about giving signatures to anybody. The Israeli post is requiring signatures for most parcels that come domestically or internationally. In Tel Aviv at least...
2. When i expect a letter, i take all the drugs out of my home and hide them somewhere. I clean everything that might be indicating that i'm a drug user so EVEN IF police decided to make a little search in my home they wouldn't think i have any connection to any letter with drugs inside.
3. Don't order anything from flagged countries like NL, India and China.

Besides,
some people think that customs here are super though and x-rays and dogs everywhere and bla bla bla... It's bullshit...
Customs here are the same like anywhere else.

I never had any problem with police/customs and i never knew personally someone who got arrested SR related, but from the bits and pieces that i collected it seems that for small amounts of drugs in the mail they might try to give you a phone call and ask you if you were expecting any mail, or send you a "love letter", or ask you to come to their office to take your mail, and then bust you.
I think that in some cases they might send 2 cops to your house for a check but i really don't believe they do this every time they catch something.
This is true only for small personal amounts. For bigger amounts idk how far they might go to catch you but i'm pretty sure that first they'll put you in custody, take every phone, computer or cash from you, and only then ask you the questions. By then you probably better be with a lawyer.
If cops ever come to you, you should pretend to be complete innocent, and don't give any information that might be indicating that you were waiting for the package. There's a good chance that if your house looks clean and you play your role fine they won't even go inside and just leave and forget about it.

Good luck and stay safe!!
