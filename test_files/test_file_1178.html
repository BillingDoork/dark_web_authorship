One more question though. Let's say I'm ordering a package from France to Italy (that's not what I'm doing, but let's say it is). When I write the address out with the country at the bottom, should this read 'ITALY' or 'ITALIA' or should it be the french word for Italy?

ITALY (all uppercase), it's the best. Use italian names for everything else but the country, that you spell in english.

No matter from what part of the world the letter comes, when it's international the country is usually spelled in english or, at the very least, in the DESTINATION country's language; this is the typical etiquette of shipping letters (that you can find, btw, in sites as RoyalMail or USPS etc.).
