Title: Re: Writing Return to Sender on package when received
Post by: malacath on October 22, 2012, 07:27 pm
I'm not sure about the law specifics, but the idea is that anyone can send you anything in the mail without your consent or knowledge, by writing return to sender you are providing evidence that you have no idea who sent the package or why so your plan was to return it to the sender, thinking it was a simple mistake.

You are officially rejecting the package by writing "return to sender" on it, if LE were doing a controlled delivery (sting operation) they would watch you take the package inside, give you some time to open it and take out the contraband, and then arrest you. If you write return to sender and leave the package unopened you can say you didn't order anything and the package was not yours and that you planned on bringing it to the post office and having it sent back. Again I'm not sure how it would hold up in court but it certainly looks better than having the police come in while your snorting a line of powder next to an opened package that they knew contained drugs!

The reason you wait a day is because if it is a controlled delivery, they wont wait longer than a day to storm in.

Keep in mind this is VERY unlikely to happen for personal amounts of drugs, if they were to intercept your package and find a gram of speed they would confiscate it, send you a letter, and put you on a watch list but that would probably be the end of it. Its not it to LE to make a big deal about 1 gram.

If you do get a letter, realize that they will be checking all your mail for now on.

If you are ordering larger quantities, like to sell, its a different story and the "return to sender" method wont do a thing. Its hard to believe that someone would accidentally send a pound of cocaine to you or send it as a prank, if it was a gram of coke you could say someone wanted to get a random American arrested so they sent drugs to your address, but no one would send thousands of dollars worth of drugs to a random person as a prank or by accident!
