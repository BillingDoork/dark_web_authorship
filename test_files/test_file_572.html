So the pros of USPS Box
1) Much cheaper
2) Not ran by private business and assuming they would need warrant to open it at local PO (if they didnt do so at customs)
3) Much larger volume of packages in the facility blend in better.

Con's on USPS
1) Its a PO Box this may or may not affect the customs agent and how they evaluate the risk factor of package/letter
2) No 24 hour access
3) Only one name can be used to on the box


Pros of UPS Mail Box services
1) Its a street address that can be written as a suite or apartment # ( looks like a business or apartment address)
2)Can use up to 3 personal names and 1 business name
3) 24 hour access (maybe this is beneficial to get packages after hours scope out the place etc)
4) UPS signs for all packages and leaves them in the mail box area for you to get at your will
5) A have a business partner  that can apply for a job at the same location they are always hiring. *this is a big maybe though obviously

Cons of UPS Mail Box services
1) Way more expensive 1 month here is the same as 6 months at a USPS PO Box
2) Private company (assuming if a package looks shady at the UPS store or smells they would open it, notify police)

Thats all I can think of for cons on UPS but I'm sure there is more.
