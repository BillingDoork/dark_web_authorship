Title: Re: Please explain US priority postage to me
Post by: slirp on May 03, 2013, 02:05 pm
The flat rate envelopes might be $5.60 but the small flat-rate boxes are a little more.... something like $5.80 but I can't remember for sure.

The flat-rate prices are ONLY when using the "flat rate" envelopes or boxes available from USPS.  If you use your own bubble mailer then you have to pay per ounce.  I highly recommend using a flat-rate box so that there isn't any ambiguity about the price.  You may want to send a test package or at least not send all 4oz of coke at once since you're a little unsure on this.

If you mail from the USPS counter then you can get a tracking number for free.  However, they may then have you on camera.

Don't depend on plastic vacuum sealing to keep out the scent.  For that you need MBBs (moisture barrier bags).  Either way be sure not to get cocaine residue on anything in the package (eg from cocaine dust in the air, touching package with fingers that have cocaine on them, etc).
