Title: Re: shipping weed to US po box
Post by: Wazup7 on November 22, 2012, 02:38 am
Shouldn't be a problem.  Just use a box that is big enough to receive the package (to avoid having to pick it up in person).  Work with the vendor to get smaller packages if you need to.  Cannabis vendors on SR are generally very good at stink-proofing their parcels--I have never received a weed order that I could smell inside the package.  In fact most weed orders I've received don't even smell inside the first vac-seal (except probably to dogs).
