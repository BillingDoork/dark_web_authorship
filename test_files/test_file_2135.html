When a vendor uses the "Bulk Reply" option, the message still appears as unread in your sent messages folder.
Therefore it's very possible that the package is indeed in transit.

Auto-finalize is set to 17 days after the vendor marks the package in transit. This is the case for all orders on the site. If it still has not arrived after about 13 days, put the item into resolution requesting a time extension of 5 days. It annoys vendors no end when a person puts the item into resolution a couple of hours before auto-finalization.
If you ask for an extension of 5 days, this means that the item will next be seen by an SR Administrator 22 days after the item was put in transit (the time extension begins after the auto-finalize period expires). It will not auto-finalize once in the resolution center, but you will have the option to finalize at any time if you do receive your package. If your package does not arrive within a reasonable time period and / or repeated time extensions (I always give it up to a month), you can then request a refund of X % in the resolution center.
The 'resolve' option becomes available 10 days after the vendor marks the item as 'in transit'.

Note that your buyer stats will be affected if you let an item auto-finalize, or if you are issued a refund.
