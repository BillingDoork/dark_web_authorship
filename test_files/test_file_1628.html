Post by: rynoragin on August 20, 2013, 02:29 am
I don't have any experience with that my friend so I don't want to give you bad advice, however when you go to the post office and receive the package just wait a while before you open it...maybe even up to a week. Also, you should ask the vendor if they require a signature. That will help you determine whether or not it seems right. Also note that USPS has to have a warrant to open your parcel. So if a postal inspector talks to you and asks you to open the package you can refuse and he has to come back with a warrant so have your house clean and the package gone. FedEx and UPS don't need warrants though so keep that in mind.
