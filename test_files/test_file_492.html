P.O. Box or Rented Mailbox with Fake ID

-another layer of anonymity
-disposable
-much higher consequence if you're caught
-requires interaction with someone f2f

Home address

-"plasuible deniability" depending on your laws, location, etc.
-no f2f transactions
-hassle free receiving (ideally)
-not quite "disposable"
-home is up for investigation or a seizure worst case scenario

pick your poison.
