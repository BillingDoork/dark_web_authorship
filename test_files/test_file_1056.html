Title: Re: 2 diffrent Vendors said tracking confirms delivery, but packages haven't arrived
Post by: Shroomeister on May 05, 2013, 04:13 am
2 points here:

1) As a vendor I can tell you I have gone through this with what I believe to be honest buyers. DCN says delivered, but the buyer is empty handed. Usually the package turns up at the door with in MAX a couple more days.

2) Since USPS has changed the Tracking system back in January DCN (Delivery Confirmation Number) to the new "Tracking 400" slips, there has been even more of these types of cases and even more issues.

We have seen some #s not show up in the USPS system at all; some are right on point; some only appear in the system AFTER the package has been delivered....etc

Suffice it to say all is not lost....yet ;)

I would give it a couple more days and see if the packages do not just magically appear. Assuming of course you AND the vendor are being honest.

I would not be worried about anyone "knocking on your door" or anything of the like (although I have not real evidence to back this up)

Unfortunately if at the end of all this you still have no packages, the DCN/400 slip says "delivered" and your order ends up in resolution the chances are that the SR admins will side with the vendor and release your funds.

Not taking sides in any of this only trying to remain objective and state facts.

Best of luck to both you and the vendor involved. Keep us updated if the package arrives in the next few days.
