Title: Re: Signature required?
Post by: stenr on July 16, 2013, 08:33 am
Normally I would say signing for things isn't worth it for tracking but with my experience ordering from the EU (I also live in Canada) I can say that I would almost rather sign for my packages then have to blindly wait for them. If it makes you feel any better, I have had 2 orders from the EU, one took about 20 business days and the other has yet to arrive and it has been about 22 business days. However, every other purchase I have made has made it quite promptly (A week at most). The fact that one of them has shown up, leads me to believe the shipping times from certain parts of EU to certain parts of Canada is just really, really slow.

Just what I think, anyone else from Canada with experience ordering from EU please share what you think as well as I'm still fairly new to it.
