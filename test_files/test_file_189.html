Title: Re: Need some help
Post by: jagfug on November 27, 2012, 08:34 am
Just talking about this on another thread. I can only tell you United States laws, and we have "Plausible Deniability" - Means exactly what it says.

It's completely plausible that you have no idea what was coming in the mail.

They would have to have a search warrant. If not, then even if they find a pound of heroin, they violated your rights, if there was no warrant signed by a judge.

I would advise against "Return To Sender" - That sounds like the WORST idea! - Now they try to return it. Find the return address is fictitious. Then have probable cause for the postmaster to open it, because it will end up in the "dead letter" bin. It will be under scrutiny.

They will put two and two together, and your friend will probably be under surveillance for a while

Really, just don't do that. I'm not sure how you thought that would work, maybe I'm missing something, but it sounds like a terrible idea.

Just accept the letter/parcel .

Shit I've signed for packages standing right in front of my house. There was heroin or coke inside. Good packaging.. Been buying since April. Don't be afraid to ask for help.
