Sorry to hear you lost your money mate, did a little digging yesterday and this diplomatic package scam is a pretty common one for nigerian scammers.

Get yourself some bitcoins and do some searching on the rumor mill part of the forum for reputable vendors that sell what your looking for and place an order through SR with them. Never give out any personal details to anyone except your vendor and always make sure its pgp encrypted when you send your address or any sensitive information.

If you ever need any help with sorting our encryption, getting bitcoins etc just look around the security sections of the forum, there's a few guides there and if your still stuck just make a post on the forum and someone should help ya out :)
