There are many variables here.

1. Did you Finalize Early? (sounds like you have but just confirming with you).
2. Did you use your real name or a fake one?
3. Which country do you live in?

If you Finalized Early, your only remaining hope is the vendor doing something for you on their own accord. Unfortunately, once an order is Finalized, it's Final. SR won't intervene as the transaction is complete as far as they are concerned. This is the reason you should always use the Escrow system. If the vendor insists you have to FE, ask for your order to be cancelled and go with another vendor who allows the use of Escrow for newer buyers. If a problem occurs during your transaction and 10 days have passed since your order was put in transit, you then have the opportunity to open up a dispute in the Resolution Center, where you can either request a time extension for the delivery of your order, which effectively will delay your payment to the vendor until your item arrives should it turn up a little later than expected, or request a full or partial refund. SR Support will look at any evidence and make a decision on the matter if the vendor and yourself can't reach an agreement.  :)
