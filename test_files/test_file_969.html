Title: Re: My dilema with P.O box's
Post by: wiggum on April 16, 2013, 12:38 am
UPS/FedEx are frowned upon because 4th Amendment doesn't apply to them when they are the shipper, so they can search any package for any reason.  However, I think it's a completely different story if their only role is to be the PO box.  Maybe check out the UPS store (or other PO box service store) terms of service and see what it says about opening packages.  Maybe a better idea is to get a PO box from an independently operated store.

Shipping via US mail to a UPS store box is probably pretty safe because it is a federal crime to open someone's mail.  If you ship via UPS/FedEx to a UPS store, they can open any package they want to for any BS reason they can come up with.  No one working at a PO box store is going to open someone's US mail, that's a serious crime.  The most they would do is call the cops if your PO box seems to be making the entire building reek of weed. :P
