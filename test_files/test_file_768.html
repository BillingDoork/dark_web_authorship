Title: Re: Did RM open my mail?
Post by: MrVidalia on February 24, 2013, 12:38 am
If they want to monitor your mail they will do it in a way that is hard and likely nearly impossible to detect.

The postal service of larger industrialised nations processes quite a large volume of mail. This is accomplished by automated machines. Sometimes, exactly as stated, items are posted but not well sealed. Other times machines jam, the alarm bells go off, someone unjams the machine, tapes up your letter or package and throws it in the correct bin.
