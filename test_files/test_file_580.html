Title: Re: My item did not come?
Post by: zerik on January 26, 2013, 07:01 am
2 days is late is a little soon to panic. Do you know for a fact what day it was shipped?

It depends on a lot of factors. For domestic priority shipping shouldn't take too long to ship. It depends on where you and the shipper live. Also believe it or not on the size or shape of the box.

I would not wait 30 days. There is no way a domestic priority mail will take 30 days.

Are you using an address you control? Is the name you used to a real address? If you used a fake name or a bad address there are all kinds of things that can go wrong. Non of which are the vendors fault.

If you used a real name to a real address and isn't in a large or weird  shape than most places will be received in 3 days from the shipping date. There are a few states where shipping can take 5 days.

Since you stayed in escrow and said you are dealing with a vendor with good feedback you probably don't have to worry.

You have to factor in the weekend as well. There are some legit reasons it might take a little longer. Remember this isn't just any package. The shipper has to have time to take care with packing as well.

If you do get in touch with the vendor don't assume anything yet. It is a little soon to be in panic mode. Just ask if he could please let you know when he/she shipped so you know when to look out for it.

Hope this helped you.
