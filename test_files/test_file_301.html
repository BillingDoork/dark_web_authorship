Post by: AusGuy on October 31, 2012, 03:27 am
Just to comment as someone who has spent substantial time in KL (captial of Malaysia), drugs from a government level are very strict.

Trafficking has mandatory death sentence even for foreigners or expats. With that said though, it's only for what they deem trafficking quantities. I think like 14g of heroin and then it goes up from there.

They do take small amount very seriously (maybe a couple G) if you get busted and can end up in a pretty shitty jail.

To add some points to the other side, I know personally know people who take shipments in KL. Always small amounts (I'd never suggest sending weed there) and always very carefully. The reality is the Malaysian government has worse customs inspection than most western places (although getting better and still better than most of Asia). And they're still very unlikely to peruse random small personl shipments they catch in the mail (like every other country). On top of that corruption is pretty rampant in the police force and you'd be surprised what a bit of money will get you out of.

So, never ship anything over personal use. If someone wants to risk personal, that should be their choice just like every other shipment off SR, because it doesn't mean automatic death as they'd have you believe.
