Post by: Rocknessie on August 20, 2013, 12:32 am
The worst-case scenario is they raid your home and find enough gear in your home to charge you with Intent to Distribute. They will NOT need a warrant as they will say they have Reasonable Suspicion (of illegal activity), as such they don't need a warrant.

They don't do controlled deliveries. A UK pill buyer/reseller was busted when customs intercepted a package, the police simply turned up at his door.

Therefore you should clean house.

Remove all traces of drugs and (and this is the hard part) also clean out your computers. BY THAT I MEAN MOVE THEM TO A DIFFERENT LOCATION. Take them to a friend's house. You simply do not want your computer equipment seized, it's hugely inconvenient and any evidence of Silk Road could be taken as evidence, and if you have any dodgy porn or torrent films or music they might just prosecute you for that if they can't get a drugs charge to stick.

If you clean house they have no EVIDENCE of any crime. Nothing was in your possession. There is no proof you ordered anything. They might, no - will, know what you were up to, but it's POSSIBLE an enemy of yours sent that package in order to incriminate you. The police might know in their heart's that's a crock of shit... but if they can't prove otherwise that's Reasonable Doubt right there.

Do not act like a gangster, grinning and taunting them. Not not admit anything. Act surprised. Act innocent. Act slightly aggrieved at the entire situation.

"I have no idea what you're talking about. I have never seen that package in my entire life. This is the first I have ever seen or heard of it. I don't know what else to tell you."

When they start talking about non-evidential stuff, that is "well who do you think sent this, and why?" type of lines, say "I can only speculate". Speculation is a legal term - and something that is not admissible as evidence". You can speculate that it's an (unknown) enemy. You can speculate perhaps it's someone who fancies your girlfriend. You can speculate it's someone who you've rubbed the wrong way but has kept a quiet grudge. You can also speculate it's the police.

The most important thing is to ensure they have no evidence of any crime. So clean house and never confess, not even a little bit.
