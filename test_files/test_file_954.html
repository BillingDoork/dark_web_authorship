No, this is not about mylar bags, this is about something I haven't seen too many people complaining about.

Many vendors still think that "stealth" only means something that cannot be seen from the outside of the packaging. Yes, it's a minimal definition.
But hey Vendors, customs officers & post people have hands. They can touch things like you could touch my dick, if I'd let you.

This means, if:

1) your product (or something suspicious) can be felt from the outside, it is not "stealth". I've received so many letters where you could feel a ziplock bag or pills or else, without too much of an effort.

2) If upon opening the package, the product can be easily seen,  then it's not "stealth".
