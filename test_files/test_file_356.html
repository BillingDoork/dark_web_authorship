Post by: Rowsdower on December 08, 2012, 03:05 pm
I'm assuming you're talking about weed or something, not things like benzos or prescription drugs.  I don't think it's a great idea to put it in the checked baggage, despite vacuum sealing, since on an X ray it will show up as organic matter inside of plastic packaging which could seem suspicious and if it's perceived a big enough anomaly to warrant enough suspicion airport staff might open it up and look inside.

If I was going to smuggle weed I'd always body pack it somehow, I brought just over an ounce with me once for a vacation which was vacuum sealed up a few times with a machine and then just strapped to my chest with duct tape.  It was still quite risky doing it like that because if I would have been patted down someone could have felt it, but I made sure to wear clothing with absolutely no metal bits anywhere so that the metal detector at least wouldn't go off.
