Title: Re: Question About Undeliverable as Addressed
Post by: veiove on December 27, 2012, 05:57 am
I called USPS as I'm in the same situation. As soon as packages are deemed undeliverable, they are returned to sender. If the sender didn't put a return address or if its invalid, USPS will OPEN the mail in hopes of finding a return address receipt inside or a way to contact the owner. If the packages are worth more than 10USD and can't be returned, they will be sold.

Assuming you shipped narcotics or forgeries, USPS will file a police report.
