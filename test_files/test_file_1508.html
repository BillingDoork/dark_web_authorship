Music festivals are well known for having an abundance of people selling drugs that aren't what they say they are. MDMA is often methylone or another cathinone, increasingly NBOMes are being sold as LSD. If you want to be sure that what you're taking is the real thing, it's very wise to bring your own drugs to a festival.

To the OP, if you are 100% confident in the stealth of your vendor by all means have the vendor ship it to the hotel, if you have any doubts and a good handle on stealth shipping yourself, just do it that way.
