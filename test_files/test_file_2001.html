Liquids in the mail are scrupulously checked by postal workers and customs because of the threat of liquid explosives by terrorists, they are particularly stringent in the U.S because of 9/11 and 7/7. The only viable way to ship drugs in liquid form is doing it for trafficking amounts and even this is fairly difficult and it takes months to prepare the set up needed.

Stick it in the mail like an ordinary letter. It will be fine. Rule number 1 is don't try and think of complex ways to do things that are already simple to do, when you try and be clever when you don't have the experience to be clever then you end up getting nicked and that certainly isn't clever.
