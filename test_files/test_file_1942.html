Same thing happened to me recently.  If there is no delay, you should be fine (they need to go through the process of getting warrants, etc if they are going to attempt a controlled delivery).  And if they ask for an ID just tell them you lost it and ask if they will deliver it again or just leave it in a safe spot.  That's what I did and they gave me another "we missed you" card the next day, which was enough to get the package at the PO.  Most importantly, make sure you know your mailman because if it's not him/her attempting to deliver it, your f-u-c-k fucked (unless you deny delivery).

being friendly and putting a smile on your face goes a long way...because I'm pretty sure the blond at the PO wasn't supposed to give me it without proper ID, but I got it.
