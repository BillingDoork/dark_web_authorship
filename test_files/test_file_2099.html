Title: Re: Printing Labels for Express shipping
Post by: th3creeper on August 24, 2012, 06:56 pm
Quote from: DoctaFeelgood on August 24, 2012, 05:23 pm
How do you seasoned vendors go about this? Just print up the customers address on a plain blank paper and then calculate shipping and apply the right stamps? Or is there a better way?
It's best to avoid Express shipping but if you must...ship using the flat rate envelope($18.95) or small Express flat rate box as this will save you from having to calculate postage.  The only way to be able to print out an Express label is to pay electronically(credit card/debit card, etc) for postage which obviously leaves a paper trail.  If you have a card that can't be traced back to you it's doable but I strongly discourage anyone from ever sending drugs with postage paid electronically.  SO if you must ship Express..you must use Postal Form-PS Label 11-F (Express Shipping Label)which are available at any PO.  You can fill out the form yourself, tear out customer copy..affix Express stamp or equivalent to envelope and drop in any USPS blue drop box.
