If you receive a seizure letter, just shred or burn it and pretend it never happened. Eat the loss and move on. More than likely you won't be pursued by law enforcement. If you are, they will most likely attempt to deliver the package as you expected it to be. Then shortly after arrest you for trafficking it. If you're sure that's the case, don't ever accept the package. If asked, say you aren't expecting any packages, it must be a mistaken address. As long as they can't prove you conspired to do so through other methods(computer forensics, etc.).

You couldn't be more right mate. As a rule of thumb that i'm comfortable following: 10g or less you can order to a home address. Anything above either try splitting the order up over the course of a month and stockpile the shit. Don't get greedy and reckless.

If you want to place an order like 100g then you should be able to afford to invest in a safe drop and cover the cost in the margin you make when selling.

Put yourself in the shoes of LE, what is the minimum amount they would consider before considering knocking on your door, then play the risk/reward game I.e. what amount are you prepared to order that will make the risk justifiable?
