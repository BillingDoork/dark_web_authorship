Post by: quinone on November 04, 2012, 05:42 am
NL to Canada 100% success, same with UK and China and Lithuania.

US to Canada ... a little more troublesome.

Canada custom's often holds onto your package, but it's often cuz Canada Post is backed up the ass, it's not something i'd be worried about.  Had several orders with a hole poked in em (usually a hole punch), but as long as you're buying from good vendors that doesn't matter, they poke it near the envelope flap.

Their love letter's really ARE love letters.  That is to say, they are very kindly written packet of information on how to be careful about purchasing prescriptions online (the situation may differ if you're buying an ounce of blow, I wouldn't know, don't do coke, heroin or meth, no thanks :) ), and that the letter has been sent to you simply to inform you that your package is being returned.  Not asking you to call/contact anyone.  BUT it may differ if like I said they seize an ounce of C, if it looks like prescriptions they figure its online pharmacies.
