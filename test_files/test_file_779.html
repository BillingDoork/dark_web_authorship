Title: Home delivery vs. PO Box vs Private Mail Box (UPS Store etc)
Post by: vektor the pirate on February 26, 2013, 09:38 pm
I am very curious as to what your opinions are on the safety of the various places we can have packages delivered to.  I personally have my stuff going to a personal mail box not ups store but something very similar to that.  I know all the people that work there and if a package requires a signature they sign for me and hold my stuff behind the counter.  The reason I went with personal mailbox was because of the fact that if a controlled delivery was going to happen, LEO would have to stake out the store and wait for me to come.  This could take days etc after the package was initially delivered.  I don't think they would sit around this long to do this.  In my opinion they would probably call me and ask me to come to pick up the package.  I would think a USPS postal inspector would dress in plain clothes like the rest of the employees and try to get me to sign for package when I arrive there.  I know all the employees here and it would be obvious as soon as I walked into the door if it was some guy behind the counter that I have never seen before.  That would be my que to walk out and never come back.  If I am never in possession of the package how could I ever be charged with possession of its contents?

I know that some people say they don't like the thought of there packages being at the mercy of the workers at these places.  I completely understand that.  I would say if you watch reviews and the stealth and packaging methods are sufficient you should be confident that your product will not spill out or smell through the package.

I think an ideal set up would be to find a place that accepts packages and allows rentals of mail boxes and use a fake ID and cash to rent the box.  This way your identity is not tied to the box in anyway.  If you get sketched out or something out of the ordinary occurs just take it as a loss and find another place.

I fear very much having a package seized in customs and law enforcement attempting a controlled delivery.

What do you guys think is the safest delivery locations (home, PO Box, or Personal Mail Box)

Enlighten me!
