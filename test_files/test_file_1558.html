Title: Re: should I be worried yet? Express mail late
Post by: semi_feral_human on August 06, 2013, 09:34 am
You shouldn't be worried at all. In fact, you shouldn't even have expected it today.

Express mail has a Saturday cutoff of noon at most Post Offices, and to my knowledge isn't accepted on Sunday at all.

Chances are the vendor received your order on Saturday but by the time it was all enveloped up the cutoff time had passed. I think some vendors mark stuff in transit when it's packaged, not when actually shipped.

Assume it was actually in the USPS system Monday, and will be delivered today. Or if you or the seller is out in the boonies, Wednesday. If by 3:00 pm Wednesday you don't have your order the seller is dragging their feet and you should reflect that in your feedback considering you paid out the ass for express.

Good luck to you sir.
