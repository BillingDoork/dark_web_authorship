Post by: ODB on September 03, 2012, 12:02 pm
Hey guys
It all really depends what you are ordering and what risks you want to take.

First of all if you are ordering the package to a PO Box registered in your name or to your own home address it really doesn't matter, both addresses are linked to you, the beauty about the whole system is that it is very hard for LE (Law Enforcement) to prove that you personally ordered that package, and will be considered a waste of time and money to gain a search warrant for a small amount of drugs, even if they did gain that search warrant, anyone dealing in larger amounts will have no evidence on there PC (it will be stored on an encrypted USB drive with a password that is almost impossible to crack) and no drugs on the property, the only evidence they have is a letter that has been sent to an address which you can easily deny that you knew anything about it. There would be no evidence for LE to make an arrest and the cops know this. Your average buyer that is purchasing personal amounts is generally safe, if LE does show up at the door, just deny deny deny!

I order my packages to my own address, i only order personal amounts of goods, if i was to order bigger amounts of good i would find a different method of delivery and that is because if anything was to happen and i did get a knock at the door i wouldn't want to involve the other people that i live with, even though i know LE wouldn't be able to do anything more than question me.

One more important piece of information!!

If its your first orders i would order small amounts from a domestic seller, not having to go through customs is a very safe way of doing this.
