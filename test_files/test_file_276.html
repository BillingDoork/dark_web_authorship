1. Re: living with your parents just bite the bullet dude and get it delivered to your home - make sure you're at your letterbox before your parents, otherwise just say the contents were a necklace or something from your ex-gf or another fairly light object.

2. Here's what I do: The very minimum you will need is an online banking account, with one of those PINsentry devices.

a) sign up for a mtgox account using a disposable gmail/hotmail/tormail account.
b) look up the FAQ for a UK bank wire deposit - write down the sort code, account name, number etc and your mtgox account number.
c) sign into your online banking account and make the payment addressed to the above account name and details
d) 1-2 days time you will see the amount credited in your account, now you can buy some bitcoins
