That might not be such a bad idea - there are many safe and secure methods for doing that. It's not about selling out of your house, it is about the potential for them searching your house on the back of an intercepted / controlled delivery package. If you have unaccountable cash, unfortunately the police can and will seize it until you can prove where it came from. They will state that it is the proceeds of crime and link it to their investigation if they decide to prosecute. The reason they do this is because seized money, even if unrelated to an investigation, gets added to their budget if not claimed after a certain period of time.

Cleaning house means removing ALL evidence of illegality - stash the grinder as well.

Regarding the car, if it is parked on the property then yes, they will be able to search it under the terms of the warrant. If it is parked off the property, they will likely have to get a warrant to search it but will most likely come armed with that making it pointless (and even more incriminating) to stash anything in your car even if it is parked off-property.
