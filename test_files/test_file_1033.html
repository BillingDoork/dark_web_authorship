Hi,

I wouldn't worry about it.  If it's borderline I'd be surprised if they didn't just deliver it anyway.  You are not the first person from SR where their package had incorrect postage or some other small mistake.  You would be surprised at the number of packages that are still delivered even with incorrect zip/postal codes, mispelled addresses and so on.

Your package isn't going through customs, so it isn't going to be subjected to the kind of inspection that customs packages go through.

Just keep in mind what you will read in the forums, time and time again, don't sign for anything that you weren't expecting to sign for.  If for any reason you do get a visit (which is highly unlikely, you are more likely to just get  a letter telling you they found restricted items in your package and it was destroyed) from someone asking about the package, deny, deny, deny.

If you have a tracking number, check it from a computer other than one at your own home. Go to McDonald's or something.  The tracking should show you if there is a problem.
