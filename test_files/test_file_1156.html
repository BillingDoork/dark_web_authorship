Post by: lucymylove on May 28, 2013, 02:47 pm
In modern airports they usually have all tools to detect even microscopic amounts of drugs, they can take sample from surface of your fingers, bags, hairs.
So if I wanna enjoy my stuff on some sandy beach, I keep in mind the next:
1. Measure very little amount of stuff, so that you can pay penalty fine, not to go in jail. (e.g. in my case it's 150 mg of 4-AcO-DMT)
2. Prefer "effective" drugs with little effective dose (e.g. hiding 1 mg of LSD crystal, is easier than hiding 150 mg of 4-AcO-DMT, hiding 150 mg of 4-AcO-DMT is easier than 1 gram of MDMA)
3. Wrap and seal stuff several times. Spread powder evenly in sealed baggie. Wash sealed baggie with pure Alcohol in gloves before several days of flying.
4. Places of hiding, I prefer to hide in checked baggage, because it's a very big bag with wheels, it has advantages of hiding.
Among places of hiding, I prefer wheels and handles. Clean handle or wheels with pure Alcohol with gloves on.

4.1. It's a bit of tedious work to hide in wheels, but it gives plausible deniability, by saying that some stuff can be caught by your wheels.
4.2. Among handles I prefer the top one. Why us that ?
If you saw X-ray in Airports, you must think a little in what dimensions it works.
First, X-rays go horizontally, not vertically. Second, X-rays holes in airports have such size, that big bag can be put there only in two positions, either horizontally as usually it's placed, or on one of the sides, but not vertically.

Thus if you placed your thing baggie with stuff inside top handle, there's almost no chance to see that thin baggie inside handle with X-Ray
