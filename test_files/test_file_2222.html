Hi blue1,

If the orders are still marked as 'processing' then they have not yet been sent out yet by the vendors in question. When they are sent the order status will change to 'in transit'.

Silk Road gives vendors 4 days to process an order, after which point the 'cancel' link will become active and you can cancel the order if you feel that the vendor is taking too long to process it. As far as your messages are concerned a lot of vendors receive a hundred or more messages a day, and time spent reading and responding to messages is time that could otherwise be spent packaging up orders. Give your vendor 24 - 48 hours to respond to any messages, and if you want to cancel the order when the 'cancel' link becomes active then you can do so: your buyer stats will not be affected.
