Title: Re: Shipping bud Canada->Sweden & Greece
Post by: MissNatural on July 10, 2013, 06:00 am
I have seen some vendors from Canada package bud uncompressed in MBB, concealed within a legitimate product that was then rewraped with an industrial plastic wrap machine to appear as if it came directly from the factory. A plastic wrapping machine may be quite expensive.... but there are many other ways you might could do it. With bud, if they x-ray it, they will most likely find it, though they also might not. If you disguise it legitimately enough as a real product and make it look untampered, then it should get through just fine.

I personally don't believe all mail is x-rayed, unless it's a very strict country like Germany. I also have never shipped to Greece or Sweden so I don't know how good their customs are. Just do the best you can to disguise it within/as a legitimate product as possible. I personally do not believe bud ever has to be compressed if packaged correctly... besides nobody likes compressed weed.... so ugly.
