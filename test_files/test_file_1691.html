Title: Re: Arrogant scamming buyers
Post by: MissNatural on August 31, 2013, 03:43 am
This is one of the issues with vending. If you do not offer tracking then you are responsible for lost packages and if you want to be a good vendor you will offer a reship/refund. Sometimes you just gotta take a buyers word for it, and refund them and never sell to them again due to the risk of lost packages. These sorts of losses are part of vending. You could always impose a strict policy on your page and all your listings saying all buyers agree to so and so upon ordering, and state it very clearly. It may drive some buyers away, but at least you can say they agreed to finalize no matter what.

Ultimately you can't just call a buyer a scammer for claiming to not receive a package. Have policies in place, and be prepared to deal with these things.
