I'd say yeah you are being a hard ass

Don't FE, PERIOD !!!  If that means you can't get gear, then that means you can't get gear.  I live in Canada so I know what it's like to be shut out by most of the SR vendors (though Aussies have it way worse), it's frustrating as fuck and there's a buncha stuff I can't get access to.

But if you agreeably finalize early, you chose to do it.  Don't do the crime if you can't do the time.

Only way i've been able to deal is with diligent patience, cuz eventually a vendor will pop up with what I want that will ship to me in my whacky 'international' country Canada without FE.  Build up a REALLY good buyer profile and many vendors are flexible on FE if you can prove your honesty.

This whole post is sort of hypocritical because I have my own policy requiring certain users to FE, but it's because us vendors can get screwed just as easily as you.  Especially if a buyer with 0/0% places an order.  Too many scammers, it's too easy to create a shill account, order a bunch of stuff, and then demand either through resolution centre or through personal threats to the vendor that the vendor re-imburse the scammer.

So yeah, I don't like FE, won't FE on purchases I make, but completely understand why vendors ask certain buyers to FE.  Also, some buyer's irresponsibly finalize a week in or so (before receiving their package) cuz they anticipate it will be there any day, thus defeating the entire purpose of escrow.
