Title: Tip for CANNA/LARGER product Vendors
Post by: budlite49 on December 28, 2012, 11:36 pm
I've noticed on my last few orders that vendors are using USPS "Flat Rate" Boxes more and more which are priced for items weighing up to 70 lbs. I typically order bud so to put small amounts of bud/pills/anything into a flat rate box that barely weighs a pound seems like a bad idea to me. I could see using flat rate if you are buying huge quantity. But USPS also offers plain "Mailing Boxes" (free of charge - they just have to be shipped priority) that are exactly the same size as the "Medium Flat Rate" but come out to be much cheaper and then you just pay by weight. (USA I'm talking here.) I think it looks somewhat bad putting a q/p h/p etc into a flat rate box that is priced to hold 70 lbs. I don't care about pricing. This is more for stealth/security. I ordered a q/p and received it in a medium flat rate that weighed almost nothing. Just seemed like it would throw up a small flag to me being that the product was so light yet in a flat rate box. (Also the box was stamped Fragile by the post office and came to me beat to hell like someone pressed on it to see what it might have been.) USPS "Mailing Boxes" all day... or non USPS boxes all together are the way to go!!! Just my $0.02.
