Not sure if your vendor has a DCN for your order? That could clear a lot of things up. It's the only way I new there was an issue with my zip code.

I know its easy and probably safer to be paranoid, but small domestic shipments are hard to get caught with...unless it was a very suspicious package.  Are you sure your vendor sent it on the day you are counting from? Vendors will mark it in transit but not actually drop for another day or so.

Just to share a little anecdotal story with you - a couple of years ago a buddy who hooks me up with some of his pills sent me some via UPS overnight while I was away on a work project. At the time, neither of us thought much about the fact that what we were doing was illegal, but I get a package to my hotel and you could literally here the pills rattling around in the package.  He just tossed a pill bottle in an envelope and off it went! Since then, with minimal care, I've successfully received pills through the mail or UPS a number of times to cities all over the US. It is typically pretty safe....just not always predictable.
