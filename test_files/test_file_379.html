Hi stiffneck,

I've read your other posts, and I'm sorry to say it certainly doesn't look like you're ready to order anything from SR.

The reasons why I say this:
a) You may not even be on the right site (you gave a seperate URL)
b) You appear to be unaware of PGP
c) You don't know your PIN
d) From the most cursory of investigation, I'm pretty sure I know your first name (you signed a post with an abbreviated version of it)

In a nutshell, you have a long way to go.

I hate to say this, but you are also acting in a way suspicious of LEO; posting unrelated URLs, encouraging the use of PrivNote (a suspected honeypot) and your terminology is odd.

Before you're ready to purchase, you should:
1. Understand the basic workings of TOR and it's limitations for privacy
2. Understand how to get bitcoins into your account anonymously
3. Have a SR account (where you know the PIN...)
4. Be able to use (and USE) PGP to encrypt your address and other messages
5. Have a safe delivery method/plan in place

Educate yourself on these things, and best of luck.
