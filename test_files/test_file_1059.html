Title: Re: What drug is the safest to ship?
Post by: anonaddict on July 07, 2012, 07:01 pm
It depends on where here is. All most all drugs should be safe for shipping as long as the vendor has taken the correct amount of precaution in sealing and concealing the product. LSD should be the safest but if it had been packaged improperly than it may send out a red flag. So I would do some research with the actual vendor. Ask them if they had a problem sending to your location, and ask them how safe their packaging is. Also look at their feedback. Sometimes customers will actually put good information in there.
