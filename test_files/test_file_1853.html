Title: Re: U.S.A bound customer question: should i ONLY order domestic????
Post by: flaxceed on July 22, 2012, 02:21 pm
USA customs is extremely lax.  I have been shipping there for 14 months without a single issue.  Look at the turmoil in the mail system right now- the push for a 5-day week, early retirements, closing sorting facilities.  They are obviously going to be doing the same volume with less people and fewer facilities.  Now does that mean mail will get more scrutiny than before?  Yes, you guessed it.  And the employees that are left are going to be even more pissed off than they have been in the past.  If you expect disgruntled employees to operate at 100% efficiency you are naive.  If you expect disgruntled government workers to operate at 100% efficiency, you are delusional.  The post office is full of people who are simply putting in time until the day they can cash out and go fishing.  There are loads of them!  I am not saying that there are no postal inspectors who are gung-ho law and order types, but they are few and far between.  Mostly they are bureaucrats at heart and in performance.  Push a bureaucrat to do twice as much work and see how that works out for you.
