Post by: Frylexa on July 24, 2013, 07:30 pm
Hey Uno,

Under no circumstance should you ever be providing identification while shipping domestically. That said, people ship cigarettes all the time with relative ease. You just show up with everything well-packaged, say in a priority mail flat rate box, labeled with the correct postage, and hand it over the counter. You can figure out what it would cost to mail whatever sized parcel via the USPS website. There's also still a few post offices out there without cameras, too. It's a matter of finding these places and appearing confident.
