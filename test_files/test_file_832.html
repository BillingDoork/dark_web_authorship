Yes, there's no need for a vacuum sealer if you have mylar bags and an impulse sealer. Vacuum sealing is good for sucking the air out of packages of cannabis, or to suck the air out of a package of pills so they don't rattle or grind against each other. Vacuum sealing does nothing to prevent drug sniffing dogs from detecting it. Only something like a mylar bag sealed with an impulse sealer then scrubbed off with isopropyl alcohol is going to make your parcel scent-free.
