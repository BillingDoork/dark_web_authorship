Title: Re: Postage to the UK from inside the UK
Post by: BDF on January 06, 2013, 02:38 pm
I really wouldn't worry too much. Check out my UK buyers thread and make sure you order from a reputable dealer who packages their product properly (dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=11298).

If you live with parents / housemates, I would be more concerned with them intercepting your mail. That, or a dodgy postman.

Royal Mail ships hundreds of millions of packages every year, the chances of an internal package being confiscated are minimal. And the police generally don't arrest people for a gram of cannabis. As long as you are not on probation or a drug cessation order, you have little to fear.

With regards to having an order shipped to an alternative address, I'm afraid that I have little experience in this regard. However, many sellers won't post to a PO Box. If you have it sent to a friends address, you also stand the chance of them or their housemates ripping you off.

I have stacked up nearly 40 orders on SR in the last year from all over the world; every single one has arrived to date. Just use your common sense and make sure you vet any seller on the forums before placing an order. If anything 'feels' suspicious, take your money elsewhere. At the very least, don't finalise early (Although you'll need to build up a decent purchase history before you can flex that right).

Either way, good luck!
