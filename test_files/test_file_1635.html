As for sitting in customs for a while then moving again. IMO I think that is safer than it ziping through in 2-3 days here is my logic (which does have holes in it)

If a pack sits in customs for a long time usually it because its backed up they are rushing to get things through so the chances of your shit getting searched heavily is low.

If the pack is in customs in 24-48 In and out feel pretty good about this too they are feeling lazy your pack got through under the radar etc.

If a pack is there for 4-5 days and comes through you could still be good but I feel like this is this highest chance of CD (if your ordering something like 1/2 or full kilo) since they got it in day 1 searched it day 2 or 3 saw what it was organized a task force for CD day 4 and push the pack out day 5 watching its every move.
