If you need the product to be shipped to your home address and want to be reasonable safe, you have to keep your house clean of any illegal substances or equipment used to consume those substances. You could just keep it at a friends home. Then make sure that your vendor sends the package without the requirement of signing it. So you will just find it in your mail box and bring it inside. DONT open it yet, but cross out your address and write "return to sender" in red. Leave it like that for a couple of days.
After a few days I would feel safe enough to open it. Remember that should the police ask questions never tell them that you bought anything!

This may sound a bit scary but are just some precautions for the worst case. Normally when the customs would find a small delivery of illegal substances you would just receive a small notice and that is all. But then your address is flagged and you should not use it anymore to buy drugs.
