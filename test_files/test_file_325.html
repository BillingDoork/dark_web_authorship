No. Tracking it using anything other than Tor would be dumb. There's no way for the tracking site to magically know you are using Tor. It would simply look like a standard Firefox Browser request from a foreign IP (Normally US, Germany or Netherlands) . Maybe you were on holiday? Nothing amazing or suspicious about that.

Using your normal computer and IP to track a package would be the dumbest thing ever.

Based on an average for most countries, customs hold your letter for about 10 days, although this wildy varies, form the UK where I am it can be 1 day, to Russia where I've seen it be 40 days before. I've got a sneaking feeling however that Finland's customs is going to be more efficient than Russia though, if you catch my drift  ;)
