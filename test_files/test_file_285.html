Title: Re: Lets talk express mail.
Post by: anex45 on November 06, 2012, 04:13 pm
I figured I would chime in with my Express experiences incase any of the info helps anyone out.

I order items via USPS Express A LOT...some from SR and some are just regular items I ordered online and I have yet to have a bad experience using Express.

In my area, all the Express packages come Overnight/Before 12PM next day no matter where they're shipped from (I live close to a huge airport and USPS sorting center). I've never had a package not arrive before the guaranteed time although sometimes they do cut it close (deliveries at 11:47AM or 11:53AM, etc...but a lot of the times I will get them earlier like around 9:30 or 10:30AM). I have had a vendor ship me an Express package on a Saturday a few times before so obviously it's not going to get there overnight and the 2nd day delivery by 3PM is selected for Monday...I have always received those packages before 12PM regardless.

A special carrier delivers all the Express mail in my area, I've never seen my regular mail man deliver any Express packages to me (he usually doesn't deliver the regular mail until between 12 and 4PM). Infact the other day I had 2 different Express packages delivered on the same day and 2 different special carries delivered them about 20 minutes apart from each other.

I've NEVER had to sign for an Express package, most shippers will waive the signature (especially SR shippers)...the package is either left at my doorstep or it is left in my regular mailbox.

Anyways, I love Express but it's important that you use a vendor who knows what the fuck they're doing when it comes to shipping Express packages.
