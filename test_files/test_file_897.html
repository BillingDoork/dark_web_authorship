No customs between states. Only at the mails first point of entry into the USA when arriving from an external U.S territory. I believe Alaska & Hawaii don't get inspected however the U.S Virgin Islands and Puerto Rico do.

The International Service Centers that sort the mail are  - Chicago, New York, Miami, Los Angeles & San Francisco. So your overseas orders will go through one of these places depending on where it's coming from and how it's transported.

I don't have any information regarding where European orders go to. It would depend on the country it comes from and how they pre-sort the mail. One of two scenarios would happen - either it's pre-sorted in the country of origin by region. IE West Coast/East Coast/Mid USA etc and then sent to the nearest center to that. Or it's all sent to the East Coast - New York, maybe Miami, maybe Chicago then sorted and sent from there domestically.

I hope this helps a bit.
