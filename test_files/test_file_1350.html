Title: Re: LSD+Summer heat
Post by: Iconoclastic on June 29, 2013, 06:13 pm
Honestly, I think it's a MUCH smaller problem than people make it out to be.  I got tabs from 3Jane in the dead of summer last year that I can't believe were degraded in the slightest.  Many other respected members on here have also mentioned this.  There was an article about a 50 some odd year old vial not kept in any special environment (amber glass, though, yes) that had practically zero degradation.  I've kept tabs in places I shouldn't have for a year+ and never had a problem.  Left them in a car baking in 100 degree weather for most of a days once and was kicking myself up and down for being so stupid... until I ate some more and realized they were unharmed.

If you think lining the box with foil isn't going to cause unwanted attention, go for it.  Hell, shove some dry ice in there at that point.  It never hurts to be cautious.  But in this case, I think your caution has a higher probability of raising suspicion (probably not a lot, but I don't think the mail carriers run into foil lined boxes very often) than improving/preventing the decaying quality of the tabs.

If anyone has a different opinion, please chime in.  I'm just going off what I've experienced.

Whatever happens, have fun with the tabs when they do show up!
