Title: Re: Waiting and starting to worry
Post by: energyimport on March 06, 2013, 01:55 am
In my experience there is not pattern or educated guessing when it comes to international shipments. They just come when they come. If two items that look weigh and feel identical are sent out t the same address at the same exact time, it wont make a difference on if they wil come at the same time. Some take longer than others its just how it is.

Once i package is on a sorting belt there is no control over what gets sorted in what order, it just happens. And as mind racking and annoying as that is its the truth. Not to say dont be careful and safe. I good amount of paranoia is always healthy in my book. But the simple fact is that if your tryign to decode a tracking number's information to see if its a controlled delivery it just wont happen. Just always remember never sign when you didnt expect to sign, keep a clean house, deny and play dumb, and keep a really good lawyer on speed dial if your worried.
