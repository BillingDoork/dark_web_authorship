LSD has to be one of the easiest items to ship. How many blotters to an envelope? Well if the blotters are the size they used to be in the 80's/90's then I'd say you could fit 500-100 in a 24x12 business envelope. As for sending it to your business address... if your business is in anyway linked to your name, and the letter gets intercepted then they're going to think you ordered it.

I have no idea about customs/police where you live but you really want to be finding an anonymous drop for your mail.  Friends/family/neighbours are still risky.

In my opinion, shipping to your business is as dangerous as shipping to your home address.

That said, in my country, if I were to be caught with a few blotter of acid <10, then I wouldn't be in too much trouble. A slapped wrist probably. 500 tabs would get me a conspiracy to deal rap more than likely.
