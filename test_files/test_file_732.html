I would tread very carefully....Whenever I see the words 'bulk' and 'Australia' in the same sentence I always laugh. The reason why our domestic prices are high, is because it is hard to smuggle narcotics past our borders, this no doubt you already know. Bringing in 'bulk' would require some sophisticated planning which by the looks of it, you have not done.

Quote
and the P.O Box gets scanned or some shit
In America, postal address' are linked with names, but in Australia they are not, once your parcel has passed customs Aus Post/courier company will most likely scan the tracking bar code so the tracking is updated for the buyer, but names shouldn't pop up.

Regardless if you've got Dick Head or John Citizen on the parcel, if it is taken for further inspection and contraband is found then no matter what name is on the front it is redundant. The person who goes to collect that package will be under scrutiny/possible investigation. Considering its bulk, I'd bet you will get more than a customs letter....

I believe fake PO boxes give a false sense of security, if authorities have identified contraband in envelopes they can simply leave a 'please collect' slip and nab the offender at collection. Using a false name on a package is unlikely to work as most require photo I.D upon collection. You could purchase a fake I.D but then your just adding more charges to your name if the worst were to happen. If I was you I would scrap this whole idea altogether. Do a little more reading, and perhaps start with some smaller shipments testing alternative methods other than PO Boxes, before ordering your 5000KG's of mdma.
