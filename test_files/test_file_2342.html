Title: Re: Has customs upped their game?
Post by: Wadozo on September 28, 2012, 06:09 pm
From my experience, some vendors really pay attention to detail and package items in such a professional manner that it's just incredible. On the other hand, there are vendors who literally don't give a shit if your package arrives or not, probably cause a lot of their buyers have FE for them anyway. Trial and error, through your own research, is the best way to narrow down the list. I hope your aware of the world wide crack down of illegal and counterfeit goods purchased online and being shipped to countries world wide, which is currently under way, called OPERATION PANGEA V. It's a major, annual operation, conducted by Interpol in conjunction with Authorities in each country, which doesn't conclude until 3/10/12. There have been a few threads started here with all the details, should you wish to do some reading on it.
When you find a vendor who is always looking to improve there shipping methods, stick with them. You know that they are putting in the effort to ensure delivery, something I really appreciate.
