Title: Re: Getting 2 lbs of weed shipped weekly to my house
Post by: fredflintstone on December 12, 2012, 03:47 am
Just make it look normal and dont tell ANYONE your doing it through the mail and you will be fine.

IMO using your house to receive 1 package per week is smarter than using a drop if the drop is just in your name anyways. People receive packages at their home ALL the time and the weight involved here wont mean a massive package, just a mid sized box. Postal workers arent proactively looking for drugs, they stumble upon that shit. So just act normal and they wont give a shit. Its a 9 to 5 job for them, they deliver your mail and go home. They arent cops, they dont wanna be cops.

Dont rent a mom and pop shop mail drop to receive 1 package per week and nothing else, that looks fishy and those places WILL be proactive to make sure their locations arent being used for drugs.

Dont tell any associates that your receiving packages through the mail and destroy all packaging as soon as it arrives and that way if anyone is busted/you have a falling out with your crew, they cant have anything on you.

I have worked with customers who receive LARGE packages through the mail and their drop hasnt changed in over 2 years. Just play the part and make sure your working with competant shippers and you'll be ok.

Do check your proceeds of crime laws though like I mentioned .. using your house may be alot to risk if it can come up for seizure. Contact a lawyer and get a real legal opinion.
