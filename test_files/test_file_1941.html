Title: Re: Tracking packages
Post by: Shroomeister on August 04, 2012, 05:44 pm
assuming you are buying. Ask your vendor, but do not be a pain in the ass about it.

Most vendors have tracking on your package, but they all seem to feel different ways about it.

Almost all of them will NOT give you the tracking #.

Most of them will not check on your tracking number for you until X amount of days after it has shipped.

Some will not check it at all and only use it if you dispute receiving your package.

Start by reading your vendors profile and see what their approach to all this is.
