Title: Re: What time does mail usually come at?
Post by: Regicide on June 12, 2012, 05:28 pm
I order a lot from QC to ON and it comes in 1 out 2 days from urban area to urban area. If you or the seller lives out of a major city center, expect up to 4 days.

As for checking your mail, I have received Xpresspost packages later than my usual mail (same day), at 4-5pm. Since Xpress is priority, this could also happen to you, so I would suggest waiting til around 5:30-6:00 in case it comes a bit later.
