The answer to your question(if taken literally) ...is NO.  It is not illegal in the US to send a valid, legally filled prescription through the mail or by courier (domestically only) providing of course, the sender is licensed to do so by the DEA.  Obviously, if it's legal to send it's going to be legal to receive as long as the prescription was written for you.
