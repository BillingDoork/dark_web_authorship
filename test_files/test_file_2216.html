Title: Re: How do you recieve products anonymously
Post by: Regicide on June 17, 2012, 03:11 am
To add to ThumbsUp's post:

Aside from selecting a trusted vendor, you also assume some risk when ordering. Your product may be intercepted and seized, and consequently may or may not be flagged (more likely with non-pharmaceuticals). In this case, you'll likely only receive a notice of seizure letter, and it is recommended not to attempt import again without testing your address to check whether or not it is flagged.
