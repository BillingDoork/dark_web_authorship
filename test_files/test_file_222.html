Title: Vendors pleas take advantage of Holiday Season, make your package Christmasy
Post by: jagfug on December 02, 2012, 03:43 pm
In case you haven't thought of it, I thought that if you're shipping, especially bigger than an envelope, or either way. You should get a hold of some Holiday stickers. Santa Claus, Happy Holidays, etc.

This time of year they are innundated with these types of things, and are a lot less likely to pay attention to one with Frosty the Snowman, or Santa Claus stickers all over them.

Just a friendly tip. Someone may have touched on it somewhere else, but it doesn't hurt to be reminded.

Happy Holidays to all!
