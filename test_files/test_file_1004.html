MDMA has a strong odor which drug dogs are able to detect. From most orders I've done on here, the MDMA has come in one vacuum sealed bag as a minimal precaution. This in itself will not prevent a dog from sniffing out the MDMA if it ever comes to that point, but the majority of MDMA orders come in non-suspicious envelopes/boxes and probably would not be flagged. This is especially true for orders that don't have to go through customs, as in your case. Do note however, that vacuum sealing alone does not eliminate odors and smells will eventually permeate the seal. Using heat sealed Moisture Barrier Bags will provide more adequate odor suppression.
