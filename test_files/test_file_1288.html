Title: Drop box less safe than home address?
Post by: pakchoi23 on June 19, 2013, 09:43 am
When ordering small amounts I believe it is safer to order them to your own house (if your house is clean). Why? Deniability. You can deny a package coming to your front door, say you didn't order it, you have no idea where it's from. If you go to a drop box then you are specifically going to pick up a package. No denying that. Unless you order some cheap ebay item and hope it arrives at the same time, (or post something yourself.)
However I think for large orders it's maybe worth the risk.

Thoughts anyone?
