Title: Exorbitant charges for registered mail
Post by: metacontxt on September 18, 2013, 04:17 pm
I'm currently having issues with a vendor over a non-delivered package. At the time of purchase, it struck me how wildly expensive their mailing charge was if you wanted a registered, traceable item; while their snail mail rate was relatively cheap. I hadn't seen that before, and of course I went with the cheaper option. But recently I've noticed more vendors doing the same thing; offering relatively cheap shipping rates for untracked packages shipped overseas (a few dollars) and ridiculously high rates for tracked items ($100+). Many go out of their way to state in their terms and conditions that they won't refund or will only offer a small refund in cases of non-delivery if package isn't registered (traceable) mail. Yet they make the shipping cost of this so high that no one would pay for it. I can't help but doubt their intentions. Is this a new trend, or am I just noticing it because it recently affected me?

Or is there some other reason why a vendor would charge so much for registered mail?
