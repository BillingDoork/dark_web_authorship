Title: Important information: Shipping USA to EU

Hello riders,

so in the time on the road, i learned a lot and had a lot of experience now.

So last time i ordered from USA, i noticed the fastest shipping time ever oversee. Only 8 days, so really great time!
But the seller had forgotten to show the bill on the letter/package.
So the EU customers couldn´t proof the transfer of paying and the paid tax...

So letter didn´t arrived at my door, only the information letter from my local customer service.
If i have a bill and a document where would be showed the paying transfer, i could go to customers and get the letter...
So i didn´t have the chance to get it.....


On the backside of the letter is an extra tip :
Please inform on future orders outside the EU, the sender that the bills must be attached to the outside of the shipment. This allows, in most cases, pre-clearance and delivery of your shipment can be made via the delivery service to your door.


So follow this information and you didn´t get such problems.
Thank you a lot!
