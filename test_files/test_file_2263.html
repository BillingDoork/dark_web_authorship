Title: Re: Large orders from US/CANADA
Post by: vladmir on September 17, 2012, 10:33 pm
Hello,


I am a new vendor from Canada , I do not have any listings yet as I am waiting on proper packaging materials to arrive. They should be here within one or two weeks, do not listen to people who claim that vacuum sealing with regular ziplock bags is fine, by the time the package gets overseas they will be STINKING, at least to a dog's nose. You really need heavy duty packaging to ensure that if a big order passes by a dog that it will go undetected, however it is statistically quite unlikely your package will be near one fortunately. If you are worried about the police, then I would suggest getting something that you do not have to sign for, and leaving the package inside UNOPENED for 24hours, because if they bust in they can't really say you knew what was inside !


Vlad
