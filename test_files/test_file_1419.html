For general postage advice/costings you should probably just consult your local post office. We don't even know what country you are in or sending too.

For advice on how to ensure whatever you are sending remains undetected, the best way is to order from a vendor with a reputation for success and study their packaging.

Just remember: must be generic/clear/flat enough to pass all auto sorting features, and have no smell. That's about all you need.
