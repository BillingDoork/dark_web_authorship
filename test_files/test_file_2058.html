Post by: Jonny Bench on August 25, 2012, 03:49 pm
UPS/FEDEX/ETC Can open your mail at any time.
Your best bet is using the automated postal service for stamps and dropping your pack off to them right before posting. If your box fits in the drop box inside you'll be good to dorp it in. If not you'll just have to take it to the desk.
Of course if you use the automated you better be using someone elses card and not your own, I also like to keep my face hidden while picking up stamps. I wear beanies, fake hair, some glasses that kanye west would approve of. All kinds of stupid shit that makes me look like every other idiot in the area but nothing at all like myself.
I do this at night, our post office is open 24hrs for electronic stamps.
This is how I do it and I've sent off well over 1000 packs in the last few months with no issues. Some of them larger but most smaller
