Easy for the postman to steal a letter while walking his round. Most of them are more interested in getting all their mail posted so they can finish their shift though.

There are alot of temps directing the mail that the automatic character recognition machines have trouble with (bad printing / font).
20% at a guess will get sent to the wrong regional distribution centres.
Some will get sent to ireland by mistake to be opened as they have no address on them. (but in your case will not be opened as the address is there, someone just pressed the wrong button).
Some will be stuck in the bottom of a bag/trolley that has been hurriedly emptied  until that bag is filled up and emptied again.

Many reasons.
