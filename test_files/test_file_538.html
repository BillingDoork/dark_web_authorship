If by "green stuff" you mean you are concerned about odor, just make sure the vendor uses at least a double vac with a mylar layer.  Some vendors triple vac, I saw one vendor doing quad vac.

If this information is not posted on the vendor's home page or in the listing, then send the vendor a PM. Explain that you have a cluster mail box and office situation & that you are concerned about odor. Ask if they use mylar.  Odor may begin to leach from food sealer packages after a few days, mylar gives you longer.  If your vendor is using mylar I say no prob to ship to a home address. Good luck.
