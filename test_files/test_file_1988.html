Post by: nobstar on August 12, 2012, 11:03 am
hi mate

to answer some of your questions:

- i use my real address. i adjust my name so that it's close enough that the postie will recognise the error - but far enough away so that i'm not indictable. an example off the top of my head - if your name was Sam Adams, you might put your real address with the name Adam Samuels. (not a great example, but you get the idea). obviously don't get it delivered to your parents house.

- yes, i think there's just too much risk using a completely fake name. posties are pretty dumb, i'd hate to ever lose a package because it was a name the postman didn't recognise.

- i've had 2 orders shipped from overseas (Canada) that i'm confident weren't a scam (there was one other, but i think i was scammed) - only one got through. whether or not it's worth getting through customs depends on what you're buying. if you're buying an ounce or less of pot, just buy domestic. it'll be more expensive, but your package won't go through customs, and you'll have it in about 3 days. but there are other things that might be worth the risk. for example, if i wanted to buy 20 eccies, i'd be taking the risk and ordering them from europe for half the price.

- hopefully you know your drugs - i'd hate to think that SR was being used by first time drug users wanting to experiment. (I'm confident this doesn't happen though, it's too much of a pain in the arse setting it all up). there are drugs that obviously aren't worth even trying to ship. obviously this is all subject to the stealth of the vendor, but heroin will usually be detected. eccies are sometimes going to be detected. but from what i've heard, ketamine won't be detected. it has to do with smells that dogs will pick up.

- for do's and don'ts - do be patient. do research on the forums and on peoples feedback. do ask if you have questions. do start off with small orders, preferably domestic ones, to understand the process more. don't be annoying. don't go slagging people if you don't have your mail in a week. don't try and order 500 pills from Europe as a first order.
