Post by: Regicide on October 10, 2012, 02:39 am
When "uncertainty" comes into play with something you need under control, it's time to consider a system with more "certainty" (certainty of no policies you may be affected by, certainty of your ability to deny, certainty of your system working enough to get your product 99.9% of the time.) Get a PO box, use a business name for it and have a business idea in mind that you're starting for smalltalk with the PO operators.

Have mail addressed to your business name (not a person's name) with the PO address. Try to order domestic, and realize that a few hundred dollars to protect your privacy and safety is peanuts when your playing on a busy Road. You're in the US and the DEA is no joke (only the fact that they exist is a joke). Do all the research you can and negate your uncertainty.
