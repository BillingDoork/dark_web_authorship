Title: Re: Questions on Receiving Shipments
Post by: abdomen on July 21, 2013, 12:17 am
Essentially, you're in a shitty situation when trying to receive any kind of sketchy things in the mail through a gopher.

On one hand, you don't want it to be someone with a lot of connections to you, who can easily be traced back to you.

On the other hand, you want to know that they're a reliable and trustworthy person.

On one hand, you want it to be someone you don't give a shit about, since you're essentially using them as a human shield to avoid prosecution - you'd want someone who was a total piece of crap.

On the other hand, you can't use someone who's a total piece of crap, because they'd just take the drugs and run. You'd need to trust them, and they'd need to trust you.

Unless you're talking about having them make cash deposits to you, to be returned upon the arrival of the drugs - but then they could simply claim the drugs never came, and essentially pay you for them with the deposit.

There are a lot of complex factors involved - this is why vendors make a lot of money.
