Title: Re: domestic/same state
Post by: jones21 on August 05, 2013, 11:19 pm
good luck--just did the same-
-read the shipping thread on the forums--you can learn a lot in that discussion.
make certain the contents in the container doesn't rattle while in transport--use the small priority mail box for 5.60--go pick up a few to practice with before mailing (they are free) --pack the container in crumpled magazine pages so it doesn't move around when shaken--i used herbal supplement containers, stuffed with cotton-get the square shape plastic bottles not the round plastic bottles that we are used to seeing in vitamin packages--the square containers pack perfectly in the box and are readily available--i even included a couple of bottles of legit supplements also--include a faux packing slip or order invoice if you have time and want to go the extra step--VAC seal (3x)or MBB if the contents have a 'smell', and TYPE the address label and return address--you can go to Office Depot or Staples to ship if you don't want to go to the USPS office--others do it differently and more stealthily i'm sure, and the method described above forces you to give up some if not all anonymity--however, if your packaging is 'normally' packaged 'properly' and addressed correctly, and you're not sketched out--everything will go fine--it may cost you a small bit of money to do it this way and also time, but you will sleep much better and your friends will be very happy.
