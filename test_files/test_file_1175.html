Title: Re: DCN says delivered - 100% never arrived
Post by: th3creeper on May 30, 2013, 08:03 am
It was almost certainly delivered..just not to you.  There seller could have made an error in the shipping address or you could have made an error giving them the address...or it could have been mistakenly delivered to the wrong address by the mail carrier. (It could have been pinched by the carrier but chances are probably slim)Your best chance to find it will be to ask the mail carrier about it.  There's a good chance they may be able to sort it out but don't wait a day longer.  Strike while things are fresh in the mail carrier's mind.  There's really no way for the PO to know where it was delivered other than the zip code.  Also..ask neighbors if they might have had a piece of your mail delivered to them in error.  If you and the seller trust each other and decide neither of you were in error..it's customary (at least in my circles)for each party to split the loss 50/50.
