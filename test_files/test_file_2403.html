Are you buying international or domestic? What country do you live in? Usually the vendor states whether it's first class/priority/express and or lettermail. Every vendor has their own ways.

It's exceptionally easy to ship LSD. We can't get into in depth detail about packaging as their is LE creeping these forums.

Picture air mail is perfect for LSD, if not a professional business oriented lettermail. USPS hubs and locations usually have manilla envelopes that contain 2 pieces of cardboard for shipping pictures that can't be damaged or bent. Very easy to stick sheets in between the 2 pieces of cardboard in tinfoil stealthshipped further with your own twists.

The cardboard would insure none of the tabs are bent or damaged.

If you buy LSD from a reputable vendor here you will have very little to worry about. LSD is very easy to slip through the cracks due to it blending in flawlessly with legal mail.
