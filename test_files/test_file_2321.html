Title: Re: USPS Medium Priority Flat Rate
Post by: oban_18 on September 26, 2012, 02:09 am
Most newer post offices in US urban zones have a drop box just inside the lobby of the PO, with a slot large enough to accommodate that size box.  You can drop the box in the slot without having to hand it to a person behind the counter.  When I used to ship 40+ packages daily for an online business, I'd just drop them there and go.

This advice doesn't apply to you if you live near a post office that wasn't built in the last 20 years, or if you live in a rural area.
