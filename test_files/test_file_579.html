I care MORE about GOOD STEALTH packaging than I do product quality.  Usually I can get a ballpark idea from a vendor's reviews about the quality of the product, so I know about what to expect.  It's only with  three orders that I've made (out of 67, I think) that I've not been satisfied with the packaging.

Two were  new vendors that I was taking a chance on. Neither are around anymore, one of them is and it's a guy I use regularly now.

The worst was one where the guy put 2 grams of bud into a small Aleve bottle and didn't even put the top on it, threw it in a plain white envelope (sealed thankfully) and put that into a priority envelope.  When I got the envelope (I don't know how it made it to me, it STANK and was actually really good bud) and it looked like it had been bitten by a dog.  The envelope looked like it had teeth marks on it and the Aleve bottle even had a hole that looked like a bite mark.  Absolutely no idea what happened there.

Next, was one where it was a new vendor and the guy told me he "triple vacuum sealed" all his orders.  Well, it came in a ziplock bag shoved in some newspaper in a priority box.  If it had been AAA grade bud as advertised, it would have stunk.  Instead, it was 3 - 4 harvest old beasters that were totally compressed and had almost no smell.

Finally, there was an issue that I had with a reputable and prominent vendor that is active now about shipping labels.  It was just printed on a piece of computer paper, cut with scissors and taped onto the box with the corners loose and didn't look professional at all.  I was shocked because I had used this vendor before and the stealth was always good but it turned out someone other than normal was packing boxes for him/them for a while.

When I get a package I expect it to look like something is being delivered to me from a reputable business.  I stopped giving new vendors a try after being lied to about their stealth, unless they give out samples and sample reviews cite good stealth.

But yes, you NEED TO COMPLAIN! I didn't go on the forums back when I experienced it, but I gave one of the guys a 4 of 5 (bud was good quality, it got to me and he was a new vendor and I didn't ask about his stealth) and the other a 2 of 5 (weed was terrible and stealth was terrible, but it made it to me).  The other guy, I of course gave a 5 of 5 because he has been perfect every other time and it wasn't his fault.   It's your duty to let the community know what happened so that no others risk losing their freedom!

Regarding the OP's legitimacy, I of course don't know but what he is saying sounds more believable than a lot of the 'I got busted' posts.  The thought of getting a  Love Letter doesn't really bother me too much, so I don't think it's a great scare tactic if that is what it is.
