Title: Canada Post and Fake Names

Ladies/Gents,

There seems to be a fair amount of discussion whether or not you should use a fake name for delivery of illicit goods.

I wanted to let you know that this is not the case with Canada, or specifically with Canada Post. For those of you who are unaware of the policy, the Canada Post regulations state very explicitly that they must deliver all mail sent to an address, regardless of what the name on the package/letter is.

I am still receiving mail addressed to the family that lived in my residence before me two years ago despite the postlady knowing damn well that they don't live there anymore. As such I regularly switch up the names on the items I order and all of them have gotten through fine.

So, fake away!
